Gigi Hadid responds to body shamers

 Posted on September 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  233 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Getty

Gigi Hadid has used social media to defend herself against negative comments regarding her weight. After facing criticism for going against her word and purposefully shedding pounds to fit in with industry standards, the supermodel made clear that the differences in her shape are simply down to her age.

"It's called growing up," Hadid wrote in a comment to another Instagram user. "Bodies change as girls become women, just as my mom's body changed at the same age."

Getty

"I loved my body as a 17-year-old athlete and I love my body now," she continued. "Yes, I've lost some 'baby fat', but muscle mass from my intensive volleyball training in high school has changed over the years into lean muscle from boxing, and work stress can have its affect, naturally, but purposefully changing my body has never been my thing."

This is the second time the model has been forced to confront online bullies regarding her weight, before she wasn't slim enough and now they think she is too skinny.

"I represent a body image that wasn't accepted in high fashion before…Yes I have abs, I have a butt, I have thighs, but I'm not asking for any special treatment," Hadid wrote on Instagram in September last year.
