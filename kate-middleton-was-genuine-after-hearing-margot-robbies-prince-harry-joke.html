Kate Middleton was 'genuine' after hearing Margot Robbie's Prince Harry joke

 Posted on June 12, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  622 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Prince William and Kate Middleton’s reaction to Margot Robbie’s Prince Harry joke at the 2020 BAFTA Awards resurfaces online and fans think that the princesses’ response was ‘genuine’ after hearing the actress’s joke.

The Australian star’s onstage dig regarding Prince Harry after he and his wife Meghan Markle stepped down as senior royals cracked up the audience, catching his brother’s reaction on camera.

Margot Robbie’s Prince Harry joke at the 2020 BAFTA

The 73rd British Academy Film Awards, more commonly known as the BAFTAs, were held at the Royal Albert Hall in London, honoring the best national and foreign films of 2019.

The Princess of Wales, born Catherine Elizabeth Middleton, and Prince William, The Prince of Wales had a seriously stylish date night as they hit the red carpet for the event on February 2, 2020, in London.

In 2018, Harry was made Duke of Sussex prior to his wedding to American actress Meghan Markle. In January 2020, the couple stepped down as working royals and moved to the Duchess’s native Southern California.

Australian actress and producer Margot Robbie worked in a Prince Harry Joke at the 2020 BAFTA Awards and Kate Middleton and Prince William’s giggly reaction was captured on camera and has since begun to circulate social media once again.

While accepting Brad Pitt’s BAFTA award on his behalf in 2020, since he had been unable to fly over due to “family obligations,” Margot Robbie read a speech written by the actor beforehand.

Pitt won the award for Best Supporting Actor for his role as Cliff Booth in Quentin Tarantino’s tribute to the 1960s film industry, Once Upon a Time in Hollywood.

The speech had been riddled with jokes, but one joke, in particular, contained a sly dig about Prince Harry’s exit from the royal family and subsequent relocation to California.

Robbie delivered her costar’s closing joke, quoting:

“He says that he’s going to name this Harry because he’s really excited about bringing it back to the States with him. His words, not mine!”

Royals relocation joke cracked up Kate Middleton

After Margot Robbie read out the speech jokingly referencing Prince Harry, the award show’s cameras flicked onto the Prince and Princess of Wales’ reaction as they sat in the front row.

In good nature, the pair laughed along with the rest of the audience and clapped the actress onstage and Kate turned towards her husband while cracking up at the comedy.

A clip of Prince William and Kate Middleton hearing the Prince Harry joke made by Robbie has recently resurfaced on the internet and viewers have begun reacting to the footage once again.

The video shared via TikTok by a user named @princessmiddleton_ has so far reached over 1.3 million views along with more than 100,000 likes, along with a comment section full of laughter.

Viewers think Kate Middleton’s reaction was ‘genuine’

Following the viral video’s recent regain of attention, many viewers have commended the couple’s composure.

@~Princess Catherine~ provided context to the story in the comment section for those who were unaware of the situation, stating:

“For Context: Margot Robbie accepted an award on Brad Pitt’s behalf and made a joke about prince harry that William and Kate laughed at.”

A user named @Emmy noted that the two were being “good sports” about the joke as many seemed to assume they were faking their laughter out of politeness.

Another viewer named @Sabrina shared her opinion, suggesting that Kate Middleton was actually being genuine in her comment accompanied by a laughing face emoji:

“Kate’s enjoyment of that did not need to be faked.”

While it seems the royal family has taken the jokes in stride, the effects of Prince Harry and Meghan’s royal exit to the United States have reportedly rattled the monarchy.
