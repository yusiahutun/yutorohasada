Jurassic Park star Sam Neill sells his Double Bay home for more than $3.6million

 Posted on September 28, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  447 words  |  Martina Birk

Jurassic Park star Sam Neill sold his four-bedroom home in Double Bay.

The Pearce Street property was believed to be sold for more than $3.6million through a virtual auction service called Openn Negotiation, a first for Sydney's Eastern suburbs. 

The 72-year-old's stunning double-fronted Federation home features an open space living and dining area that leads out onto an outdoor terrace, and is just a short stroll to Double Bay Beach.

The two-storey property boasts an expansive back garden and alfresco dining area, which follows on from the open plan living.

Ballard Property Double Bay revealed there were six bidders hoping to snap up the two-storey property, but it was eventually taken by an Australian currently living in Hong Kong.

The New Zealand-raised actor bought the property in 1996 for $839,000 with his former wife Noriko Watanabe.  

He now splits his time between his New Zealand vineyard, Canberra and Sydney with his journalist partner Laura Tingle, 59.
