Julia Foxs Latest Leather Look Includes Assless Chaps and a Horse Tail

 Posted on August 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  317 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Julia Fox's unconventional street style is reigning New York Fashion Week.

Earlier this week, the Uncut Gems star stepped out in the city while wearing a head-to-toe leather look. The black monochromatic outfit involved a boxy leather hoodie, which was cropped at her chest to reveal an ample amount of underboob, with long sleeves that extended into Fox's signature leather gloves.

Meanwhile, her maxi skirt put a sultry spin on assless chaps. From the front, the bottoms looked like your typical form-hugging leather skirt, but the back revealed two circular cutouts over her rear and, smack in the middle, a long, lustrous horse tail.

She accessorized the experimental look with stiletto black booties and a black dog-shaped purse, which also sported its own mini horse tail.

This season, the actress has been spotted in a number of boundary-pushing outfits.

For instance, she attended the Jonathan Simkhai runway show while wearing a metallic denim-on-denim look consisting of a reflective black denim jacket and a matching micro miniskirt with a raw hem. She paired the pieces with furry knee-high boots in a silver-gray hue.

Theo Wargo//Getty Images

And last week, Fox partied with Saks Fifth Avenue while wearing an oversized gray bomber jacket and a matching maxi skirt with a slit up the front. Underneath her outerwear, Fox upped the ante in a cropped plain white tee covered in shredded rips. Accessories further heightened the drama via a black choker studded with large silver spikes, black platform combat boots, and a black baguette-shaped handbag.
