Reality TV star Kim Kardashian has narrated that her eight-year-old daughter takes a swipe at her house every time they disagreed.

What North says about Kim's house

Kim shared that North West criticises her home decor and calls it ugly, thinking that she makes her mother feel sad about making her angry.

Her revelations were shared on People Magazine after she shared them on Ellen's new web series bringing mothers together to share what motherhood entails in their day to day life.

North writes mom note on toilet paper

The Forbes billionaire recently shared a sweet moment that she had with the eldest of her four children.
