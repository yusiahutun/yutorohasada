Pedro Rivera Jr. is the eldest of the Rivera brothers. He was born on April 20, 1964, which means he is 58 years old (as of 2022). Pedro is a pastor and singer by profession. Currently, he is serving as the pastor at First Love Church, located in Long Beach, California.

The pastor is famous for the several Latino worship music albums he has released since he debuted his career in 2002. Some of his notable albums include Yo Te Agradesco and Yo Le Alamo de Corazon. The pastor has been married to Ramona for more than three decades, and they are blessed with four children.

2. Gustavo Rivera

Gustavo was born on March 1, 1966, and is 54 years old (as of 2022). He has also followed in his father's footsteps to pursue a career in music. Gustavo has released several singles, such as Put My Head on My Shoulder, La Balanza, Amigo Mio, and El Billete. The singer is happily married to Martha Duarte, and they are blessed with two kids.

3. Lupillo Rivera

Lupillo was born in Long Beach, California, on January 30, 1972. He is 50 years old (as of 2022). Lupillo is also a famous singer and songwriter. At the beginning of his career, he strayed away from the music path and opted to become a restaurateur.

However, he resumed his interest in music when his father hired him to work at his studio. He started recording his songs. Lupillo has received several awards and nominations, including a Grammy award for his album titled Tu Esclavo y Amo. He was married to Mayeli Alonso from 2006 to 2019.

4. Juan Rivera

Juan was born on February 22, 1978, and he is 44 years old (as of 2022). He is a famous singer and actor. Juan debuted his music career at the age of 16. He released his first single titled El Atizador in 1996.

Some of the tracks he has released, including La Lampara and El Ser Equivocado, have topped the Latina musical charts. Juan is also an actor and has featured in several TV shows such as Le dinastia de Los Perez and Jefe de nadie.

5. Jenni Rivera

Jenni was the most famous of the Rivera siblings. She was born on July 2, 1964. Jenni was a famous singer, songwriter, and actress. She started recording songs in 1992. Most of her songs revolve around relationships, infidelity, and social issues.

Jenni was also famous for her appearances in movies, and TV shows such as I Love Jenni and Filly Brown. Unfortunately, Jenni is no more as she died in a plane crash on December 9, 2012. However, the singer will forever be remembered for her contributions to the Mexican music industry. At the time of death, she was married to Esteban Loaiza.

6. Rosie Rivera

Rosie Rivera is the youngest of the Rivera siblings. She was born on March 7, 1981, and is 41 years old (as of 2022). Rosie is the only Rivera sibling who has strayed from the music path. Instead, she is a famous television personality renowned for her appearances in Spanish-language reality shows.

Rosie is also the author of My Broken Pieces: Mending the Wounds from Sexual Abuse Through Faith, Family, and Love. She is happily married to Abel Flores, and they are blessed with three children.

How many grandchildren does Pedro Rivera have?

Pedro is the proud grandfather to several grandchildren. Jenni Rivera had five children from her marriages. Her children are Chiquis, Jacqie, Michael, Jenicka, and Johnny. His son Pedro Rivera Jr. has four children from her marriage to Ramon. His children are Jessica, Petey, David, and Alesandro.

Is Pedro Rivera married?

Pedro was first married to Rosa Amelia Saavedra. Their relationship dates back to 1963, when they first met in Mexico. The duo lived together for more than four decades. Unfortunately, their love story ended in 2008 when Rosa filed for divorce.
