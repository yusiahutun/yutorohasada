Made In Chelsea's Louise blasted as ex Alik admits to buying 40k engagement ring

 Posted on July 1, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  482 words  |  Martina Birk

And this time, Louise Thompson was at the centre of the drama.

In the South Of France version of the show, fans watched as Louise's ex-boyfriend Alik Alfus returned, in hopes of rekindling their relationship.

However, Louise has already moved on and is now in a relationship with her personal trainer Ryan William Libbey.

Alik first has a chat with Louise's brother Sam and Jamie Laing, and of course Sam runs back and tells his sister everything.

The former couple later meet up and Alik tells Louise he just wants to spend some time with her.

Talking about their "really beautiful" relationship, Alik confesses: "I looked into your eyes and I could really feel your love."

However, Louise is still unsure about what to do, and tells her ex: "It's too much for me to handle.

"It's not meant to be this hard."

The pair meet up later, and Alik – who is now frustrated with the confusing situation – tells Louise: "If I've got no shot with you then I don't know what I'm doing here."

She replies, trying to let him down: "I love spending time with you and I want to be best friends."

But American reality star Alik starts to get nasty and fires back: "It's so cliché that you're with your personal trainer.

"You've got this amazing nice guy in front of you, I'm here."

He then reveals that he was going to propose before they broke up, saying: "I had a $40,000 ring on the way."

However, Louise bites back telling him she doesn't believe him.

Alik responds: "I know you don't want to believe that because it makes it harder for you.

"Despite your flaws you've got a guy right here that still wants to marry you and be with you."

But unhappy with Louise's responses, he angrily snarls: "I deserve to be with someone that is way better than you right now."

At the end of the episode the pair meet again.

Alik apologised to Louise for being "so intense", but she breaks the bad news to him.: "We just can't be together."

Alik admits he's heard her "loud and clear".

He then added: "I'll be fine, I don't wanna be with someone who doesn't want to be with me.

"I'm going to move on and that's going to be the end of it."

And Twitter users were also emotional about the whole situation.

One user wrote: "Sobbing over Louise and Alik #MiCSOF."

Another fired out: "Louise is such a compulsive liar you can actually tell and it's scary."

A third chimed in: "Alik man. Dont apologise to her. She's a moron wrapped in catnip.."

While other users were concerned for Louise's ex, with one writing: "Alik is acting like a lost puppy."

Alik didn't appear in the teaser for the next episode, so it looks like it really is over for good.
