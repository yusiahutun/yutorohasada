Al Mazrah Dead Drop Locations

  • Al Mazrah City
  • Mawizeh Marshlands
  • Rohan Oil
  • Al Sharim Pass
  • Sa'id City Shopping Centre
  • Al Samman Cemetery

Al Mazrah City Dead Drop Location

The Al Mazrah City Dead Drop is located in an alleyway on a small island section of southern Al Mazrah City. Look for it near coordinates F3 on your map.

Mawizeh Marshlands Dead Drop Location

The Mawizeh Marshlands Dead Drop is located near a gas station in Mawizeh Marshlands, not far from the waterfront. Be wary of AQ soldiers patrolling this area. Look for it near coordinates F4 on your map.

CoordinatesF4

Rohan Oil Dead Drop Location

The Rohan Oil Dead Drop is located beside a small building in a cluster of buildings west of Rohan Oil. Look for it near coordinates C3 on your map.

CoordinatesC3
LocationWest of Rohan Oil

Al Sharim Pass Dead Drop Location

The Al Sharim Pass Dead Drop is located beside a slightly bigger, yellow building in the southeast of Al Sharim Pass, directly north of the Airport. Look for it near coordinates G6 on your map.
