James Bergener, more commonly known for being the husband of Real Housewives of Orange County star Noella Bergener, has been flung into the media spotlight recently after he announced he is filing for divorce.

After a lot of digging, here’s what we found about Noella’s estranged husband.

Who is James Bergener?

James is a born-and-bred American lawyer who is a partner of the Bergener Law Firm in Newport Beach, California.

The 50-year-old businessman was born on July 17th 1971 in California, where he spent his entire childhood and many years of his teenage years and later, adult life.

Bergener stayed in California for college where he received his education in Law in 2002 at Whittier College School of Law. Aside from his career, James is also a family man, sharing two children, Coco and James Jr, with Noella.
