Simple house designs in rural Kenya

The cheapest way to build a simple house is to stick to a square or rectangular floor plan. Such floor plans make the construction more straightforward. Other factors include the roofing and labour costs. Once sorted, you can quickly construct your simple house.

Low budget simple house designs for rural Kenya

There are many low-cost house designs in rural Kenya. These designs are very affordable and can comprise permanent or semi-permanent buildings. If you are on a small budget, these simple house designs should aid you.

Mabati house design

Mabati houses are semi-permanent, affordable, readily available, durable, and stylish. With mabati, you can quickly build stylish one, two or three-bedroom houses. Mabati houses are efficiently designed and offer better durability than timber, especially in regions infested by termites.

Mabati and brick house

As the name suggests, the mabati and brick house has a stylish and simple design that combines a brick-and-mortar foundation with a mabati wall and roof. The design is ideal for people looking for a long-term semi-permanent house, as the brick and concrete foundation offers durability.

Wooden houses

Wooden houses are cheap, simple and stylish. These homes are ideal for colder regions like Central Kenya, where termite infestation is low or non-existent. One significant advantage of wooden houses is that the raw materials are readily available. They are also cosy and warm.

Interlocking block house

If you are looking for low-budget permanent house designs, any house constructed using the interlocking block technology is perfect. Interlocking blocks are bricks that interlock with each other during construction. Homes built with interlocking bricks are much cheaper because the interlocking technology eliminates the need for binding mortar.

2 bedroom house designs for rural Kenya

Two-bedroom houses are ideal for small families, especially those with one or two children. Building a two-bedroom house can be cheap or relatively costly, depending on the materials or house design. Here are some simple two-bedroom house designs to try out.

2 bedrooms bungalow

A bungalow is a low house with one storey or, in some cases, upper rooms set on the roof with dormer windows. This simple design features two bedrooms, a living room, a hall and a kitchen. You can modify the house plan if you prefer a more spacious unit.

Two-bedroom container house

The two-bedroom container house is simple, stylish and easy to assemble. It involves using ship containers to create a living space, thus utilizing space and time. You can choose to have your home stacked vertically or arranged horizontally.

Hidden roof two-bedroom house

A hidden roof design is ideal for people looking to stand out with their buildings. It offers a sleek and minimalist exterior while maintaining structural integrity. One significant advantage of the hidden roof design is that it can save up to 50 per cent in roofing costs.

Modern two-bedroom simple house

This modern two-bedroom concept home is creative. It features a clay brick home with large windows, front steps and tiles for roofing. This simple design is ideal for people looking for costly constructions as the clay bricks are relatively cheap.

Simple 3-bedroom houses in rural Kenya

The three-bedroom house is ideal for larger families as it can house more people comfortably. You can opt for affordable materials when constructing one, especially in the rural area. The best examples of simple three-bedroom houses in rural Kenya include brick-and-mortar houses or more durable quarry stones.

Modern three-bedroom bungalow

The modern three-bedroom bungalow is creative, incorporating architectural designs from the 20th century onwards. If you own a small plot or have the luxury of acres of land, this house design provides the best solution. The modern three-bedroom bungalow is one of rural Kenya's most common house designs.

Three-bedroom contemporary house

The three-bedroom contemporary house design features elements that reflect today's architectural style. This house design is perfect for people looking to keep up with the latest trends in construction and offers a stylish finish.

Three-bedroom soil press interlocking home

The three-bedroom soil press interlocking house is a beauty. It features a modern, elegantly finished design, with grey slate tiling on the roof. The most significant advantage of the home is its reduced cost due to the use of the soil-press interlocking bricks.

Final word

Above are the best simple house designs in rural Kenya. They include trendy and affordable designs like container homes and 1,2 and 3-bedroom bungalows. These designs are stylish, cheap, and ideal for anyone looking to construct a simple dream house in rural Kenya.
