Career

After getting sober, Majlak started doing odd jobs such as filling in for his sister in her assistant job and walking dogs. He also got a job working at a dog camp. On top of that, he also had a side gig as a freelance contributor to a news website.

In 2012, Majlak started doing photography and started his business, Mike Majlak Photography. With his business doing well, he quit working at the dog camp and started working as an event photographer. Aside from his photography business, Majlak started working as a social media and marketing manager.

His first job was at a company called Nice Guy Promotions in Milford. He later joined LoveSac, a furniture retail store, where he worked as a marketing manager. On top of that, he ran a food blog, The Essential Foodie. While working at LoveSac, Mike Majlak met YouTuber Logan Paul when he enquired about LoveSac products.
