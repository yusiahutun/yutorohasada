Gucci Havana Sunglasses Film - New Gucci Short Film

 Posted on September 22, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  90 words  |  Martina Birk

Step back from the '70s craze for a moment and rewind one more decade for this '60s-inspired short film. It pays homage to the famous Burt Lancaster dive in the opening scene of the 1968 film The Swimmer. In this rendition, a one-piece clad model dives into the water, interrupting a poolside soiree full of glamorous partygoers in Gucci Havana eyewear and attracting an interested attendee. The video, shot in Malibu, was directed by Gregory Harris and Christopher Simmonds. Watch below for an exclusive look at Gucci's short film:
