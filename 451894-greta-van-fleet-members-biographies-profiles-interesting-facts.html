About Greta Van Fleet

Greta Van Fleet is a rock and roll band based in Michigan, United States. It was founded in 2012 after the members were invited to perform at a festival in their hometown. The founding members are Kyle Hauck, Josh Kiszka, Jake Kiszka, and Sam Kiszka. Hauck left the band in 2013, and Danny Wagner replaced him.
