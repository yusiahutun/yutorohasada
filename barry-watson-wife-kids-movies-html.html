American actor Barry Watson is popular for his portrayal of Matt Camden in the TV series 7th Heaven. He starred in 153 episodes of the show, from 1996 to 2006.

Barry, who made his professional acting debut from the hit show Days of Our Lives in 1990, has also worked in the show Samantha Who? (2007 to 2009) and What about Brian from 2006 to 2007.

Along with his 39 credits in acting in movies and TV shows, Barry has also worked in the direction part of the show business. He directed one episode of TV series The Secret Life of the American Teenager in 2010, and directed two episodes of 7th Heaven in 2005, making his direction credits to two.

Barry’s Personal Life: Current Wife And Kids

Barry has been married to actress Natasha Gregson Wagner since the 21st of December 2014. She has worked in projects such as CSI: Crime Scene Investigation (2008), The 4400 (2005 to 2007), and ER (2006).
