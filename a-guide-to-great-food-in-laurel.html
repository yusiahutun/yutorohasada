Downtown Laurel's Field Guide to Eating Like a Local Laurel Mercantile

 Posted on March 31, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1042 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

“People who love to eat are always the best people.” — Julia Child

If this quote describes you, you’re in good company. Our small town prides itself on supplying a wide selection of breakfast, lunch, dinner and desert options. The best part is that most of them are within walking distance of one another, so you can have a dish at one restaurant and hop over to another for a dessert. We’ve created a list to help you narrow down your search (or make it harder to decide.)

As seen on Home Town, Lee’s is Downtown Laurel’s original specialty coffee, tea and eating spot. For the coffee lovers, Lee’s offers batch coffee, pour overs, espressos, lattes, tea, all cold drinks, and much more! They serve breakfast food, soup, salads, sandwiches, quesadillas, loaded potatoes, and low carb options. They offer a kids menu as well. Order online and pick-up or eat inside one of the most gorgeous spaces in Laurel!

Monday - Saturday | 11:00am - 2:00pm 

As seen on Home Town, Pearl’s is a classic blue plate lunch diner. Miss Pearl provides classic options like fried chicken, baked chicken, hamburger steak, porkchops, mac n cheese, fried green tomatoes, black eyed peas, lima beans, fried okra, rice and gravy, spicy or regular cornbread, banana pudding, and cheesecake. Her menu changes each day so be sure to follow her on Instagram here!

As seen on Home Town, Café La Fleur offers the perfect Cajun, French dish. They serve crawfish po'boys & other Southern-inspired eats in a quaint bistro with a New Orleans vibe.

As seen on Home Town, this little food truck makes take out food a flavor-packed and delightful experience. Their menu is as creative as their owners as they offer specials each week. Expect surprises like red beans and rice, pulled pork and macaroni wraps, shrimp po’ boys, pepperoni grilled cheeses, chicken cordon bleu sandwiches, grilled chicken club burgers. They offer various low-carb options and fit everyone’s taste. Be sure to follow along on their Instagram to see their hours and specials each week!

A small family-owned business where authentic Italian food is homemade and served fresh. Located on the beautiful Central Avenue, Mimmo’s is open daily and offers take-out and dine-in options.

What once began as a Dairy Queen in 1948 has now become a Laurel staple and favorite for American classic foodies. Try their hand-spun milkshakes, fresh hamburgers, fries, chili cheese tots and much more! Voted Best Hamburger in the Laurel Leader Call’s Readers’ Choice 2021.
