Meet Jared Gopman, sMothered Star Cher Hubsher Husband

sMothered star Cher Hubsher met her now-husband Jared Gopman at the University of Florida, where Jared was a premed major and Cher, a nursing student.

The two flirted over slices of pizza at Satchel’s in Gainesville on their first date. Reportedly, Jared was impressed during the date night, particularly because of how strong and independent she looked, and also because she was not afraid to stick up for her beliefs.

After that night during their freshman year, Jared and Cher dated all through college. Jared proposed to Cher at the end of their senior year at the Kanahapa Botanical Gardens in the coolest way possible.

On April 12, 2022, Jared left a note for Cher, where he told her to wear a dress and visit the gardens. Once she got there, a woman in admissions gave her a clue and a map. Cher found a typed poem, a fresh bouquet of flowers, and another clue at the spot marked on the map. At the last stop was Jared standing in the middle of a bridge with a bouquet waiting for Cher.

Almost a year later, on May 26, 2013, Jared and Cher exchanged their wedding vows at Innisbrook Golf resort in a huge wedding ceremony with roughly 225 guests in attendance. Cher and her mom, Dawn, planned most of the wedding, which was themed “Happily Ever After.”

As per tampabay.com, the couple left the ceremony in a white horse-drawn Cinderella carriage decorated in fresh flowers. Also, they honeymooned for 12 days in Hawaii.

Jared and Cher have one kid from their marriage. The adorable duo welcomed a daughter, Belle May Gopman, on July 6, 2019. Jared took on Instagram on July 19, 2019, to share a picture of their firstborn and captioned it: ‘Baby girl Belle May Gopman, the stars shine for you!’

Jared Gopman Age

According to radaris.com, Jared Gopman’s age was 32 as of July 2022.

Jared Gopman Job

Talking about Jared Gopman’s job, his Facebook shows that he has been working as a Resident Physician in the Division of Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery at Mount Sinai Health System in New York.

As for his education, Jared graduated from the University of South Florida Health Morsani College of Medicine in 2016. He also studied Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery at Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai from July 2016 to July 2022.

Jared’s wife, Cher Hubsher, first made her reality TV debut in 2007 when her sweet 16th birthday bash made it to MTV Network’s My Super Sweet 16 Presents. Later that year, she appeared on My Super Sweet 16 Presents: Exiled and also made her acting debut playing Tina in Blind Thoughts.

2019 proved to be a breakthrough year for her as she and her mother Dawn got through a series of interviews to appear on TLC’s smothered.

Besides being a reality TV star, Jared’s wife is also a nurse. Cher is a love guru running her own company, NYC Wingwoman, as well.

How Much Is Jared Gopman Net Worth?

While we don’t know how much Jared makes at Mount Sinai Health System, the average pay for a plastic surgeon in New York is close to $500 thousand a year. We guess Jared’s yearly income is somewhere around that.

As of 2022, Jared Gopman’s net worth was estimated to be under $1.5 million.

Jared Gopman Height

Jared Gopman Family

Jared Gopman’s parents are Bruce Gopman (father) and Amy B Rechtman Gopman (mother).

Unfortunately, on January 3, 2019, Jared’s mother passed away after a long-fought battle with breast cancer. The Gopman family held a funeral service on January 6, 2019, at Temple Beth El (2815 N Flagler Dr. West Palm Beach FL) at 1 PM in the main sanctuary, followed by Shiva at 7 PM in our home in Palm Beach Gardens.

Jared has a step-mom named Sherry.

Other members of his family include a sister, Alyssa Gopman, who was 35 years old as of 2022.

As we already know, Jared’s mother-in-law is Dawn Hubsher. Besides being a part of sMothered, she is an author as well, who published a book titled A Bond That Lasts Forever. Jared’s brother-in-law, Grant S. Hubsher, is currently an Emergency Medicine Physician at NYP In Your Community.
