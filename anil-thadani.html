Anil Thadani (3rd October 1968) is a 53 years old film director. He is the founder of AA films. AA Films is a motion picture distribution company that was founded in 1993 in Mumbai, India. Many Bollywood hit films like ‘Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania’, ‘Fukrey’, ‘Firangi’ and more have been distributed by his company.   

Family

Anil Thadani was born in a Hindu Family on 3rd October 1968 in Delhi. Anil Thadani is the son of Kundan Thadani who was also a director. To date, there is no information available about his mother, siblings, and cousins. It looks as Anil Thadani is able to keep his family out of the limelight. He was first married to Natasha Sippy daughter of the film producer Romu N. Sippy and later got divorced.

Then he got married to one of the most beautiful actresses Raveena Tandon. They tied their knots on 22nd February 2004. Soon the couple were blessed with a daughter named Rasha Thadani on 16th March 2005 and later with a son named Ranbir Thadani on 12 July 2007. Anil Thadani’s wife Raveena Tandon adopted two girls at a very young age named Chhaya Tandon and Pooja Tandon.

Education 

Anil Thadani completed his higher education and then he did B. Tech (Chemical Engineering) from the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras. He did his Masters of Business Administration (MBA) from the University of California, Berkeley. He also did his Masters in Science (M.Sc.) from the University of Wisconsin, Madison.

Professional Life

Anil Thadani started his career by working in “Chevron Chemical Company” as a research engineer. Moving forward he became the director of the company named Symphony Investment Managers Ltd and later joined as a chairperson in Symphony Investment Managers Ltd. He also associated as a director of a pharmaceutical company named Orchids Chemicals and Pharmaceuticals Ltd for five years from June 2005 till September 2010. Anil Thadani is now a director of AA Films. AA Films is a film distribution company that distributed many Bollywood hits like ‘Welcome to Karachi’, ‘Fukrey’, and many more. One of the biggest hits of Indian cinema ‘Baahubali: The Conclusion (Hindi Version)’ was distributed by his distribution company “AA Films”.
