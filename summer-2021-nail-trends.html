The Coolest Summer 2021 Nail Art Trends

 Posted on July 26, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  378 words  |  Aldo Pusey
1

Smoky Swirls

Like a trailing swirl of smoke, this abstract nail design is all over Instagram, and it'll only keep growing this summer.

2

Heavy Metal

Take your favorite neutral or nude, and upgrade it with a metallic finish.

3

Tiny Accents

Unlike all-over metallics, this trend uses the glitzy elements in smaller amounts—a sticker here, a line of rhinestones there.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

4

Complementary 3D

Texture nail art has been a thing for a minute, but we predict that summer 2021 is going to approach it from a minimalist perspective—think noticeable designs in nudes and neutrals.

5

French Plus

Your regular French tip with a 2021 treatment. Ask for a nail art design applied over a French tip; extra points if it makes a colorful contrast.

6

Rainbow Nails

Mismatched nail colors remains a trend that just won't quit, and we still love how effortlessly cool it makes nail colors we already own.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

7

Throwback Butterflies

Dua Lipa brought back the butterfly nail art trend from last year, and we're so grateful.

8

Cheeky Checkerboard

The checkerboard fashion trend will continue moving onto our nails this summer. Spice it up with extra florals, or be happy with the already intricate design.

9

Artistic Tips

This trend takes all the artistry you love and isolates it to just the tip of the nail, creating a riff on the classic French manicure.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

10

Nostalgia Overload

Smiley faces, '90s pop art, and cartoons are fair game for summer 2021.

11

Water Marble

Remember spending hours trying to perfect water marbling nail art? It's time to dust off the skill.

12

Simple Pink

All of the nail art is fun, but simple pink and nude manicures are still very on trend for 2021. (And they're so much easier to do on your own.)

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

13

Summer Brights

Likewise, you can never go wrong with a pop of neon for summer.
