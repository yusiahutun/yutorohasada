Angel fangs vs angel bites: The differences and similarities

 Posted on July 23, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  743 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Among the various lip piercings, such as Medusa piercing, Monroe piercing, Ashley piercing and more, the angel fangs and angel bites stand out as some of the most beautiful options. However, most don't know the differences and similarities between angel fangs vs angel bites. So, what makes each one of them unique?

Lip piercings can be a way to showcase your individuality or cultural identity. But beyond these, lip piercings are considered beautiful by many due to their ability to enhance facial features and create a unique, edgy appearance.

Angel fangs vs angel bites

Is there a difference between angel bites and angel fangs? Yes, angel bites piercings are double piercings placed symmetrically above the upper lip. Others refer to them as Madonna Monroe piercings or anti-bites. They're also called anti-bites because they're pierced in the opposite location of snake bites.

On the other hand, angel fang piercing is a variation of angel bites. Like angel bites, they're double symmetrical piercings above the upper lips. However, what distinguishes vertical angel fangs is that they penetrate downward through the lip, creating a distinctive fang-like appearance. Others call them paired vertical upper lip piercings.

Angel fangs vs angel bites price

The cost of lip piercings varies depending on the piercer, the shop, and your geographic location. Most well-run shops charge between $50-$80 for angel fangs per piercing and $50-$75 per piercing for angel bites. In addition, several stores list the charges on their websites; checking them out before visiting is recommended.

Angel fangs vs angel bites pain and healing time

The pain level for both piercings is slightly above average at 6 out of 10. It depends on the thickness of your lip and your pain tolerance, but it's manageable.

What's the healing time of these piercings? All oral piercings need excellent hygiene. If you take care of the piercings correctly and avoid traumatising the skin during the healing process, they'll fully heal in a few months. The average healing time for angel fangs and angel bites can be 12-16 weeks.

Are angel fangs piercings safe?

Yes, but it's worth noting that all piercings present the possibility of an infection if not cared for properly. Angel fangs are partially in the mouth, so keeping them clean is essential.

Every time you drink, eat or smoke, rinse your mouth with a non-alcoholic mouthwash. Also, clean the front of your piercing twice or thrice daily using a sterile saline solution.

Jewellery for angel bite piercings

The typical jewellery for angel bite piercings is the labret stud. The labret stud comprises a flat, fixed back and a removable, threaded accessory on the front. While labret studs are common in lip piercing, they're also typically used in ear and nose piercings.

Jewellery for angel fangs piercings

Angel fang jewellery typically resembles a curved barbell with a spike on one end. The spikes are available in different lengths, and you can find interchangeable designs to make adjustments. The other lip ring end is typically a gemstone or a ball.

What jewellery material is used for angel fangs and angel bites piercings?

Final word on angel fangs vs angle bites

Both angel fangs and angel bites are double symmetrical piercing placed above the upper lip. However, the difference between angel fangs vs angel bites is that angel fangs penetrate downward through the lip, creating a distinctive fang-like appearance.
