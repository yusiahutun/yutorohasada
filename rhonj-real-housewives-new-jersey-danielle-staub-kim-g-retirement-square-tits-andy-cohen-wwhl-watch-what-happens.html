Since Danielle's return to the Bravo series, Kim G. has taken to Twitter to call attention to some of Danielle's past humiliating moments. She also accused Danielle of renting her home and insinuated that she can't afford to own one.

When a caller asked Danielle what she thought about the "harassing tweets," Danielle replied, "Kim who?"

"I just really thinks she needs to go into that retirement home already," she said. "I mean, give it up."

Andy Cohen then brought up the "square tits" comment heard 'round the world.

"It was actually Ashlee [Holmes] that called me [square tits] because I had a problem with my implants," Danielle explained. "Thank you so much, Ashlee. That was special of you. Sweet. Your mother raised you well." (Ashlee's mother is Jacqueline Laurita, another of Danielle's nemeses.)

Danielle explained Kim G. was not the one who coined the nickname, saying, "She reiterated it quite a few times because she can't come up with her own thoughts."

When a caller asked Danielle if she thought Teresa Giudice and Melissa Gorga would ever make up with Siggy Flicker and Dolores Catania, Danielle said yes.

"I don't think it's that serious," she said of the debacle that ensued after Siggy and Dolores informed Teresa and Melissa they'd be participating in Kim D.'s charity fashion show.

Kim D. also acussed Teresa of "rekindling old flings" while her husband, Joe Giudice, is serving time in prison, which Danielle adamantly denied during a recent interview with TooFab.

"It is absolutely 100 percent not true, and it is unacceptable to even suggest it," Danielle told TooFab. "People need to stay out of her marriage. Her marriage is just fine. I've been there when she's been on the phone with Joe, and I've had the privilege of being a part of the conversation and speaking with Joe. You can see that they are madly in love with each other."
