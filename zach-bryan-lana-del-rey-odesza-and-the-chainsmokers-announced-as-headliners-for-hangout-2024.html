Zach Bryan, Lana Del Rey, ODESZA, And The Chainsmokers Announced As Headliners For Hangout 2024

 Posted on March 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  324 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

GULF SHORES, AL (CelebrityAccess) — Organizers for the annual Hangout Music Festival shared the details ahead of the event’s 2024 return, including a lineup led by Zach Bryan, Lana Del Rey, ODESZA, and The Chainsmokers.

Set for Gulf Shores, Alabama from May 17-19, the festival will feature multiple stages of music across with artists drawn from genres such as pop, country, rock, rap, and electronic.

Along with the headliners, this year’s Hangout lineup includes the likes of Cage The Elephant, Nelly, Dominic Fike, Doechii, Alison Wonderland, Chromeo, Westend, The Stews, Subtronica, and more.

Along with music, Hangout offers a respite from the winter doldrums with a full slate of beach activities, from beach volleyball and slip-n-slide fun, to a beach dance club and swimming.

As part of Hangout’s efforts to support the local community, the festival will revive its Earn-A-Ticket Program (in partnership with Propeller) which debuted last year, providing fans with a chance to take action online and volunteer their time in exchange for festival tickets and additional prizes.

In 2023, more than 75 volunteers participated in Hangout’s Beach Cleanup with the Alabama Coastal Foundation and Fort Morgan Civic Association, collecting a total of 3,860 pounds of trash from four beaches near Alabama’s Fort Morgan.

Details about the volunteer program will be announced soon.
