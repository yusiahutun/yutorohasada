Mimi Keene is a young, talented, and beautiful English actress who is popularly known for her work in the Netflix series named Sex Education. Her career began by working at The Royal Court Theatre as a Soap actress. She has also been nominated for The British Soap Award for Best Young Actor. Apart from working in films and TV series, she has also received a voice role in a game named Castlevania: Lord of Shadows- Mirror of Fate. She has also appeared in a Short Film named “The Escape”.

Early Life

Mimi Keene was born on 5th August 1998 in the United Kingdom. There is no information shared about her parents but she has a younger sibling whose name is unknown to us. According to her Instagram account, she seems to have a strong bond with her father. She did her schooling at The Italia Conti Academy of Theater Arts where she studied for 5 years. Since then she was focused and started preparing herself for her acting career.

Mimi Keene Career

Her career was started when she made a professional debut on 19th November 2010 at The Royal Court Theatre where she played the role of Janey in the drama Kin. Later she started working hard to get into the film industry. In 2013 she made her debut in various series and films such as School for Stars, Sadie J, Our Girl, and EastEnders. She has worked with Letitia Dean in EastEnders. She also had a voice role in a game named Castlevania: Lord of Shadows- Mirror of Fate.

In 2014 she appeared in the second part of the video game Castlevania: Lord of Shadows- Mirror of Fate. In 2016 she worked in the series named Casualty. Mimi gained worldwide fame in the supporting role of “Ruby” in Netflix’s Sex Education, a comedy show that hits almost everywhere in the world.

Awards and Nominations

Mimi has been nominated for The British Soap Awards in 2014 under the category of Best Young Performance. In 2015 she was again nominated for Inside Soap Awards for her work in EastEnders under the category of Best Young Actor.
