Romeo and Duet: Oti Mabuse's hottest ever snaps exposed

Strictly Come Dancing star Oti Mabuse became a household name when she joined the BBC show as a professional dancer back in 2015.

Oti won the competition twice with soap star Kelvin Fletcher in 2019, and then with comedian Bill Bailey in 2020.

The dancer as since become a celebrity detective on The Masked Dancer and a judge on Dancing on Ice.

Sheer bodysuit

Oti wore some great outfits on when she was a judge on BBC's The Greatest Dancer, including this sheer black bodysuit.

Showing off her incredible dancer's body, the outfit left little to the imagination.

The skin-tight sheer black material went from her neck to her ankles, showing off her sizzling figure.

Oti shared a picture of the outfit after the show in February 2020, adding that she felt "special".

Her stylist, Jessie Culley, confirmed it was a vintage piece.

Busty sequin dress

Oti is no stranger to sequins, and took to Instagram to show off her sensational Greatest Dancer look.

The professional dancer wowed in a sparkly mini dress, which she shared on her Instagram grid and stories after an earlier photoshoot.

Oti also thanked her personal trainer for helping her achieve her sensational figure.

She captioned the Instagram story: "I promise to stop complaining about the squats and sprints if this is the result".

Steamy swimwear

There's no question Oti looks amazing in a bikini – but this picture also shows off her athletic physique.

She captioned the picture: "When dance is your passion... everything is a dance floor."

The picture was taken in South Africa, where Oti was born.

She began dancing as a young child with her sister Motsi, who is also a professional dancer.

Sexy Strictly siren

One of the best things about Strictly is the incredible outfits the dancers get to wear.

For the launch of Strictly 2018, Oti looked stunning in a tiny pair of black shorts and a sexy black bralette.

Oti has previously opened up about body confidence, especially when it comes to having a bigger chest.

In a separate Instagram post, she explained: "I’m really pleased and honoured that me talking about having a bigger chest helps women feel even the slightest better about themselves."

Channelling her inner Beyoncé

Oti put on an eye-popping display when she channelled Beyoncé in a catwalk-themed dance on Strictly Come Dancing in 2017.

Fans were gobsmacked and had to do a double-take to make sure it wasn't actually Beyoncé appearing on the BBC show.

One fan said: "I thought Beyonce was on Strictly tonight. Oti you are amazing.”

Another astonished fan said: "I literally thought Oti was Beyonce."

Oti showed off her incredibly toned legs in a swimsuit-style outfit with plenty of glitter.

Natural beauty

We all love a bit of glitz and glamour, but Oti shows you can look incredible dressed down, all natural.

Sitting in a pink bikini, Oti looked stunning in the sunshine.

She looks effortlessly beautiful, with her natural hair on display.

One fan commented: "I love your natural hair. gorgeous."

Another said: "Love your natural hair!! Looks fab."
