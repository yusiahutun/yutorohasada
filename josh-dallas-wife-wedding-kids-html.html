American movie actor Josh Dallas is known for his role as David Nolan/Prince Charming in ABC's Once Upon a Time (2011). 

Further, he has appeared in movies and TV shows like The Boxer (2009), The Descent: Part 2 (2009), The Last Days of the Lehman Brothers (2009), Ghost Machine (2009), Five (2011), and Red Tails (2012). 

In 2018, the actor was cast as Ben Stone in NBC's Manifest. 

The actor has also appeared in the Marvel superhero movie, Thor (2011), as the character named Fandral. 

Married Life With Wife & Kids

The actor has been married to Ginnifer Goodwin since 2014.
