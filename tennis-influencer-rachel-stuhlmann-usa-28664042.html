'World's sexiest tennis influencer' Rachel Stuhlmann sets pulses racing in tiny top

 Posted on September 20, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  430 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

American tennis beauty Rachel Stuhlmann has again caught the eye with a sizzling Instagram shot that leaves little to the imagination.

The 32-year-old had already wowed fans during the football World Cup by donning a revealing stars and stripes bra as she tried to inspire the USA players ahead of their game with England. It arguably worked, with the underdogs performing impressively to hold the Three Lions to a 0-0 draw.

But the former tennis professional has now posed in an outfit even more striking. And the influencer also took the opportunity to provide some playing tips to her 237,000 followers.

Pictured in a blue crop type jumper that showed off her busty chest, as well as a short skirt, the stunning star wrote:" With all my travel, I haven’t been able to hit the courts as much as I’d like!

"So lately any time I get to hit, my focus has been on finding my timing, moving my feet, and getting in a rhythm. What helps me is early racket preparation, getting to shots early, and shot placement/depth rather than speed. What are you guys working on in your game?"

Fair to say that most of the replies weren't specifically about tennis tips though, with @matthewchamberlain 92 writing: "Wow you are absolutely gorgeous. Such beautiful eyes and smile."

Is Rachel Stuhlmann the sexiest star in tennis? Let us know in the comments section.

@kly1962 joked: "I missed the 1st return on your serve. I was so distracted I didn’t even see the racket, the ball or even the court….." while @trinkleryan replied: "Just enjoying the view."

@kathy_emu however, seemed more concerned with the logistics of Stuhlmann bouncing around the court with her big bodily assets: "Are they even real??" she asked. "How the hell do you play with them?"

Stuhlmann is a former tennis player for the Missouri Tigers and earned an NCAA national ranking of 77. She's also famed for using her Tik Tok account to promote her passion for tennis and revealing outfits.
