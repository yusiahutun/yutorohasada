The lady joined Forbes Media in 2009 as a staff writer and a reporter. She worked for Forbes Magazine, Forbes.com, and Forbes Life Magazine. Brennan was also the Forbes Video Network producer. She discussed finance and business on screen.

Brennan has interviewed numerous billionaires, heads of state, and corporate executives. Her profile story on Equity Group Investments Chairman Sam Zell was one of five magazine covers featured in the 2013 Forbes 400 issue.

She left Forbes Media for CNBC in December 2013. Morgan joined CNBC as a general assignment reporter in Englewood Cliffs. She served in the position for about five years.

Brennan contributes to finance and business news for various CNBC shows. CNBC made her a Squawk on the Street co-anchor on 2nd June 2020. The show broadcasts daily from the New York Stock Exchange.
