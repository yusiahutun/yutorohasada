7 times Victoria Beckham twinned with mini-me Harper Beckham

 Posted on April 10, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  593 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

David and Victoria Beckham's youngest has a seriously impressive style file

    It's not every day you get to access Victoria Beckham's wardrobe and wear whatever you want from her luxe designer collection - unless you're her mini-me daughter Harper Seven, that is. 

    Victoria and David Beckham's 12-year-old daughter is the youngest of the Beckham family, but her age certainly isn't a match for her unrivalled sense of style. Despite only being a tween, Harper has sported some seriously cute sartorial moments in the past, and has even bagged herself a seat in the front row of several Fashion Week shows through the years.

    There's no denying that Harper is a carbon copy of her former mum, Victoria. With her own fleet of luxe handbags, access to items from her mum's eponymous fashion label and the influence of her famous brothers, Harper is easily one of the best-dressed celebrity children. 

    Ms Beckham often coordinates her outfits with her mini style muse. From silk and lace dresses designed by VB herself, to designer denim and Spice Girls-inspired outfits, Harper's fashion credentials only seem to be accellerating as she grows up.

    Nothing delights style fans more than when Victoria twins with her daughter.

    Mummy-daughter monochrome moment

    VB and her mini style twin rocked all-black ensembles as they stepped out for another soirée in Paris last year. Harper's dress featured a detailed web-like bodice crafted from delicate black lace and a halterneck fit, boosting the 11-year-old's sartorial status to a whole new level of fun.

    Victoria and Harper in citrus tones

    If there was ever a reason to add citrus tones to your wardrobe this summer, Victoria and Harper called it first. The stylish mother-daughter duo were seen looking ultra-glamorous on a night out in Paris wearing coordinating lemon and lime-inspired outfits.

    Harper has the 'Victoria Beckham' simle

    In a photograph taken from Victoria's 50th birthday party, Harper is her double in an unseen photo taken by actress and party guest, Salma Hayek. 

    Not only has Harper adopted her mum's signature 'Posh' smile, but the 12-year-old matched her mum on her birthday in a pastel-toned slip dress. 

    Slip dress sisters

    The slip dress is one of Victoria's most signature silhouettes synonymous with her fashion brand, so it's no surprise her daughter, Harper, chose to wear a pastel-toned number to her Prada-themed 12th birthday party. 

    Victoria also wore a strappy, full length dress, opting for her favourite all-black combination to let her daughter shine. 

    Pastel-hued pair

    Victoria and her youngest looked runway ready as they posed in the mirror of a luxe Miami hotel. Harper looked angelic in a strapless waterfall gown in lilac tones, as Victoria sported a dreamy ruffled dress in pink.

    Suited and booted

    We couldn't get over the moment Harper stepped out in a monochrome power suit beside her dad David as they headed to Victoria's Fashion Week show in 2023.

    A suit is one of Victoria's most staple wardrobe items, so it only makes sense her daughter copied one of her most-worn looks. 

    Duo in denim

    Harper looked super sweet in a pair of wide-leg denim jeans on and a cloud blue leotard this year, which she topped with a delicate necklace and a slick hairstyle.

    VB launched her own denim line back in 2020, admitting that the versatile fabric has become part of her sartorial philosophy. Denim twins!
