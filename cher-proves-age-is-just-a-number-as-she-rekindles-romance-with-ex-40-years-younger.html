Cher proves age is just a number as she 'rekindles romance' with ex 40 years younger

 Posted on August 16, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  363 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Just four months after their reported breakup, Cher and Alexander Edwards (AE) have been spotted holding hands, seemingly rekindling their romance.

The music icon was seen leaving a night out with her ex-beau in Beverly Hills, along with some other celebrity friends, leading to speculation they’ve found their way back to each other.

Cher spotted holding hands with ex Alexander Edwards

The Believe singer 77, and her music executive ex, 37, were spotted getting close as they held hands whilst leaving a Beverly Hills restaurant.

Both lovebirds opted for all black, matching Cher’s gorgeous long locks. In a previous interview, she made it clear that her long hair was here to stay despite approaching 80.

Alander and Cher weren’t alone, as they’re said to have been enjoying time out with Columbian singer J Balvin and his girlfriend, Valentina Ferrer, as well as Rack City singer Tyga.

Although the reunited couple are said to have broken up in May, a source told the Daily Mail at the time that the two never really lost touch.

“She actually really cares about AE, and now that she’s closer to her kids, she wants more stability in her life. Although it’s off for now, no one in her close circle would be surprised to see them back together,” they claimed back in May.

A look back at the relationship between the two

The couple are said to have met at the beginning of 2022, during Paris Fashion Week. Months later, in November of the same year, they were photographed holding hands on a night out, again, with Tyga.

The next day, Cher took to X, previously known as Twitter to confirm the dating rumors, replying “YESSSSS” to one fan who wrote: “Somebody’s got a new boyfriend.”

In March 2023, they made their red carpet debut Versace fall 2023 fashion show in March, however, things were to come to an end just a couple of months later.

After a ton of adorable social media posts and engagement rumors, the pair called it quits in May 2023.
