The Spice Girls Reunion Fashion Exceeded Our Wildest Y2K Dreams

 Posted on April 16, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  525 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

After a long overdue comeback—the Spice Girls finally reunited at Victoria Beckham’s 50th birthday party. (With every member in attendance!) And while we’ve been patiently waiting for a Spice Girls reunion, nothing could’ve prepared us for their surprisingly on-brand party looks. It might’ve been Victoria’s birthday, but she just gave us the best gift of all—seeing our faves serving looks together once again.

After the group split in 2000, it’s been a while since we’ve seen all five members in the same room. But, Victoria Beckham’s birthday bash was the perfect place for the group to reunite in style. The Spice Girls celebrated their very own Posh Spice’s special day by performing a karaoke version of their 1997 song “Stop,” accompanied by their iconic choreography. While it definitely made us nostalgic for their past performances, we couldn’t help but focus on how chic they were all dressed.

Everyone knows that fashion is a huge part of the Spice Girls’ ethos, so of course we’re always taking note of what they wear. Each member is defined by their distinct personality and fashion sense. And as one of the biggest girl groups of the ‘90s, everyone has been obsessing over their every outfit since they first formed. From Baby Spice’s hyper-feminine pink looks to Scary Spice’s signature leopard print, the Spice Girls have blessed us with so many fashion moments over the years. 

While it’s been almost 30 years since they first banded together, their iconic looks of the ‘90s have remained a huge source of fashion inspo for it-girls everywhere—and thanks to their recent reunion, we have even more of their outfits to aspire to. To celebrate Posh Spice‘s birthday, the group dressed to the nines and somehow accidentally referenced their individual Spice Girl personas for their looks. 

  • Posh Spice, Victoria Beckham

    The birthday girl Victoria Beckham was seen leaving her party in style. (That’s, on David Beckham’s back of course.) But, her birthday look truly lived up to her posh reputation. Her sheer sea foam dress screamed luxurious and was the perfect outfit to dance the night away.

  • Baby Spice, Emma Bunton

    Emma Bunton (Baby Spice) knows how to pull off a girly look. Her party look, although still feminine, is an elevated version of her ’90s looks. Her long sleeve velvet dress has a slit at the leg and paired with white silk cuffs, she looks classy as ever.

  • Sporty Spice, Melanie C

    Melanie C’s black cutout dress is a perfect party look. As Sporty Spice, she frequented track suits and joggers in the ’90s—and this classic silhouette harps on her sporty background while still looking chic.

  • Scary Spice, Mel B

    Mel B is back to her Scary Spice roots with this jaw-dropping red number. While leopard print was her signature look in the ’90s, this gorgeous red dress brings all the Scary Spice drama we know and love.

  • Ginger Spice, Geri Halliwell

    Geri Halliwell’s outfits as Ginger Spice were by far the most iconic of the group. This elegant white dress is a toned-down version of her loud outfits of the ’90s—and we’re obsessed with how effortlessly she pulls off the bow trend.
