Meghan Markle's Something Blue Was the Dress She Wore on Her First Date with Prince Harry

 Posted on June 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  365 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

The long-awaited documentary about the royal family, Queen of the World, is finally coming to HBO on October 1, and not only does Meghan Markle make an appearance in the film, she also reveals secret details about her Givenchy wedding dress that no one knew about before.

Related Story

Speaking in the new documentary, the Duchess of Sussex opens up about her unforgettable Givenchy wedding gown, and the "something blue" she sewed into the dress for her wedding day. Looking at the gorgeous dress on display, Meghan asks the interviewer, "Somewhere in here, there's a piece... Did you see it? The piece of blue fabric that's stitched inside?"

When the interviewer answers that she hasn't seen anything, Meghan goes on to reveal that her "something blue" is "fabric from the dress that I wore on our first date," referring to the very first time she went out with Prince Harry. The Duchess can't contain her emotion when describing the detail, and when the interviewer responds, "That's about the most romantic thing I've ever heard," Meghan giggles.

WPA Pool//Getty Images

The fact that Meghan secretly commemorated her first date with her husband within her wedding gown speaks volumes about how she feels about her husband, Prince Harry—and it's a beautiful testament to the strength and foundation of their relationship.

In the TV interview the couple gave following the news of their engagement, Meghan and Harry revealed more details about their first date. While Meghan explained, "It was a blind date," Harry described his awe upon seeing Meghan for the first time. "I was beautifully surprised when I walked into that room and saw her. I was like, 'Okay, well I'm going to have to up my game,'" he adorably revealed. In fact, that first date went so well they decided to meet up again right away.

The rest is history.

The documentary Queen of the World debuts October 1 on HBO.
