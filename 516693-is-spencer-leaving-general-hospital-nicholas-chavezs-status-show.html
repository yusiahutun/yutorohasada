Early childhood 

Nicholas Alexander Chawez was born on 6th September 1999 in Texas, USA, before his family relocated to Denver, Colorado, when he was five. He is the eldest of four half-siblings. Nicholas spent most of his childhood outdoors playing and participating in sports.

He attended East High School, where his passion for acting would manifest from a tender age. Chavez was also a Colorado Children's Chorale member who got the chance to perform at Ellie Caulkins's Opera House.

After graduating high school, Chavez joined Rutgers University Mason Gross School of the Arts. However, his stint at the university didn't last long when he eventually dropped out to pursue his childhood passion of acting in Los Angeles.

Career

After leaving school, Chavez began pursuing his dream by training at the local Visionbox Studio Theater under Jennifer McCray Rincon. He earned temporary acting roles while doing odd jobs before signing under JB Talent Management.
