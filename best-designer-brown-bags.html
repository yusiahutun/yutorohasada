21 Best Designer Brown Bags 2024

 Posted on May 31, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  934 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Each print issue of Harper’s Bazaar features “In the Market,” a column highlighting the best new items of the season, shot exclusively behind the scenes in the Bazaar fashion closet. For the height of fall, senior fashion and accessories editor Jaclyn Alexandra Cohen gives us an up-close look at the season’s best investment bags, all in covetable shades of brown.

At the office this season, the Bazaar fashion team has been asking ourselves one question: Is brown the new everyday bag color?

Designers seem to think so. On the autumn/winter 2023 runways, houses from Bottega Veneta to Valentino remade their heritage bags in beautiful shades of rich, dark brown. I’ve since spotted these chocolate-hued bags all over street style and throughout the winter event circuit in NYC. The more I see them, the more I feel that these shades are a softer and more natural twist on a classic—the bag equivalent of changing your black mascara to the brown-black shade. There’s also an intriguing gradation of shades, from browns with purple and gray undertones to lighter, subtler tans. Whether paired with a tonal, all-beige look like at Fendi, or worn Celine-style with jeans, a collarless jacket, and bow blouse, these bags have been proven to have range.

Many of the season’s best brown bags came through the Bazaar fashion closet for our recent Fall and Winter print-issue photo shoots, so I curated 21 of my favorites that all serve their own purpose and function. Below, I break down the best designer brown bags across six essential categories to round out your bag collection, from wish-list investments to the ones I’m already carrying.

We all need a carryall that lives up to its name. This season, designers are providing the most luxurious takes with strong curved handles, buttery-soft leather, and all the space. Whether you go for a more artful approach with Fendi’s origami tote, or a more ’70s feel with Celine’s belted soft shopper, these Mary Poppins–esque bags suddenly feel much more sophisticated.

Whether it’s paired with jeans and a blazer or trousers, a sweater, and an oversize wool coat, a brown top-handle bag gives any outfit a polished finish. From a classic bowler shape like Hermès (a wish-list investment) to Ferragamo’s sculpted handle frame shape, these bags lean formal without feeling too dressed up. Added bonus: Theit structure allows you to jam-pack them without ruining their shape.

The Spacious Hobo Bag

WOVEN CIRCLE HOBO

Bottega Veneta Medium Gemelli Bag
Il Bisonte Snodo Line

OVERSIZE CARRYALL HOBO

Gucci Jackie 1961 Large Shoulder Bag

Presenting the undercover commuter bag for the modern woman. Whether you’re catching a gym class on the way to work or traveling to the office from a weekend away, these hobos are easy to carry and discreetly hold everything you need. My favorites celebrate brand signatures such as Bottega’s woven leather and Gucci’s heritage Jackie silhouette (but extra oversize). Their sturdy handles make them easy to carry on your shoulder; some, like Il Bisonte’s version, also have an adjustable strap to wear crossbody.

The Effortless Crossbody Bags

SUEDE SADDLE CROSSBODY

Chloé Steph Medium Double Carry Bag

Chain and Embellished Crossbody

Dolce & Gabbana Devotion Shoulder Bag

QUILTED CLASSIC FLAP CROSSBODY

Chanel Classic Handbag

Crossbody bags give you the freedom to be hands-free when walking around a bookstore or going to an art gallery on the weekends. I also love their compact sizes. Sometimes, I pack one in my carryall tote to the office, so I can swap bags if I’m headed to an event in the evening. (They’re also useful if I pop out to an appointment midday.) Chloé’s version in suede or Chanel and Dolce & Gabbana’s quilted leather bags are as functional as they are luxurious.

The Elegant Hardware Clasp Bags

FRAME-CLASP BUCKET BAG

Victoria Beckham Frame Bucket Bag

CLASSIC SHOULDER CLASP BAG

Etro Vela Shoulder Bag

TRIANGLE CLASP SHOULDER BAG

Saint Laurent Gaia Small Patent Brushed Shoulder Bag

Brown bags with a refined hardware finish are the perfect update to clasp it all together. Each one has a hinged kiss-lock closure framing the top, adding a healthy dose of vintage charm. For something more sizable, try Victoria Beckham’s North–South bucket bag or Etro’s oversize shoulder bag; they can both fit your favorite on-the-go extras. (For me, the list includes a pink Vitaminwater, a Mason Pearson hairbrush, and a travel-size Drunk Elephant moisturizer.) If you’re heading out for the night and need only a few key items, Saint Laurent’s compact shoulder bag is a sophisticated finishing touch.

Everyone remembers their first designer bag. This season, labels leaned into nostalgia by redefining the shoulder and handheld starter bags we know and love with graduated shapes and fabrics. Pierre Hardy coated its small shoulder bag in suede, while Telfar introduced a sculptural circle silhouette. Valentino balanced function and fashion with its logo-closure shoulder bag—it doubles as a clutch with a removable strap.
