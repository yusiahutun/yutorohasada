How to Pick Food Storage Containers

Food storage containers come in all kinds of shapes, sizes, and materials. Whether you’re looking for something to simply pack away your leftovers or options for lunches and travel, the choices available are seemingly endless. So. How do you narrow them down?

Most storage containers are equipped with airtight closures designed to keep your food from going bad for as long as possible. There are still some key features that you may want to consider, however, depending on your preferences, including leak-proof lids, size, and stackability. 

Perhaps the most important thing to take into account when purchasing these containers is material. The most common selections include plastic, glass, and ceramic. For those looking for an easy-to-transport material or a kid-proof option, plastic is the way to go. Others may be more concerned about environmentally-friendly or health-conscious picks, making glass the way to go. As for ceramic containers, these are great for those looking for durable bakeware that can also double as a chic serving dish. Each material has its purpose, making all three essentials in any well-stocked kitchen.
