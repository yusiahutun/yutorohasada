Mae West's diamonds in She Done Him Wrong (1933)

Hulton Archive//Getty Images

The famous actress actually purchased most of the jewelry in the film herself â€” from the chandelier diamond earrings to her wrist (and fist) full of gems. West would sell most of her jewelry to donate to the war effort, but the 1920s 40-carat diamond bracelet worn in the 1933 film remained in her collection. The stunning piece was eventually purchased by jeweler Neil Lane.
