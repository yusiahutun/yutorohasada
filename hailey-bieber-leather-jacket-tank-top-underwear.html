Hailey Bieber is getting into the spring mood by swapping her winter wardrobe for something far more weather appropriate.

The first photo captures Bieber wearing an oversized leather jacket, a cropped white tank top (seemingly the same one she wore to Coachella), a matching white thong underwear, and white ankle socks. In another photo, Bieber sheds her outerwear, wearing nothing but the crop top and undies (plus her giant diamond wedding ring).

Beauty-wise, she had a soft glam approach to her makeup, putting on subtle blush, highlighter, and a dark pink glossy lip. She finished the look off with her viral glazed donut manicure.
