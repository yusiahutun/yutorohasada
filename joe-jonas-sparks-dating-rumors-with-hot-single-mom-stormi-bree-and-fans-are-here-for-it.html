Joe Jonas sparks dating rumors with 'hot single mom' Stormi Bree and fans are here for it

 Posted on April 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  421 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

It was bound to happen sooner or later, Joe Jonas has been linked to his first ‘love interest’ since his divorce from Sophie Turner, model Stormi Bree, as the two were spotted leaving an airport in Cabo, Mexico together. Bree is a mother of one, to a daughter she shares with Lucky Blue Smith.

Joe Jonas announced his shock split from Sophie Turner in September of last year, after four years of marriage and two children. The Game of Thrones star has been spotted with ‘new boo’ Peregrine Pearson, and now it looks like the Jo Bro has also been linked to someone new.

Joe Jonas sparks romance rumors with model Stormi Bree

New Year new love interest? That’s what fans seem to think is the case for the music star as he was pictured traveling out of Mexico with the model.

The rumored celebrity couple was spotted walking side by arriving at the private terminal of the airport on Wednesday, but it’s unclear whether they hopped on the same flight.

Joe, 34, looked comfy casual as he walked along in a black hoodie and red cap. Stormi, 33, color coordinated with the singer, wearing a black tank top.

The sighting came after Joe and his brothers, Kevin and Nick Jonas,  performed at a private show at a local Cabo hotel for New Year’s Eve.

Although there’s no confirmation of romance between the two, fans seem to be here for it. It comes months after Sophie Turner was spotted locking lips with Peregrine Pearson, a British Aristocrat, after the divorce.

As the pictures did the rounds on X, formerly known as Twitter, fans couldn’t help but ‘fangirl’ (or boy) over the could-be new couple.

“A hot single mom. You know what, go, Joseph,” wrote one.

“She’s gorgeous good for him we love to see it,” commented another Jonas Brothers fan.

Another simply penned: “They’re hot.”

Get to know Stormi Bree

If this is going to be ‘a thing’ it’s best fans get to know who Stormi Bree is, although many may already be familiar with her.

Stormi Bree Henley, known widely as Stormi Bree, was crowned Miss Tennessee Teen USA in 2008 and went on to become Miss Teen USA just a year later.

In 2017, Stormi Bree became a mother to her daughter, Gravity Blue Smith who she shares with ex-partner, and model, Lucky Blue Smith.

The star has also appeared in some short movies, as well as one episode of hit TV show, Brooklyn Nine-Nine.
