Taylor Swift is 'performing demonic rituals without realizing it', claims music star

 Posted on March 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  408 words  |  Aldo Pusey

As of late, conspiracy theories have been popping up online suggesting that Taylor Swift is secretly doing demonic rituals without realizing it. 

Pop artists doing supposed Satanic rituals is, shockingly, not completely unheard of. Just last year, Doja Cat was criticized for allegedly including references to the Devil in her music. She was also dubbed a satan worshipper for her iconic red jewelry-encrusted Schiaparelli look at Paris Fashion Week. Then when Ice Spice’s upside down cross at the Super Bowl caused a stir with fans. She had attended the VIP Suite with Taylor Swift to watch her boyfriend Travis at the big NFL championship.

A Cruel Summer indeed

As fans know, Taylor has now wrapped up the Australian leg of her ever-expanding Eras Tour, and that is when she’s not cuddling up to current beaux Travis Kelce. But certain visual aids used in her performance have caught the eye of a former UK pop sensation.  

The Taylor Swift X Satan collab was given new life this week thanks to former Boyzone member Shane Lynch. The celebrity claimed to Ireland’s Sunday World newspaper even her backing dancers were in on it. 

He said: “I think when you’re looking at a lot of the artists out there, a lot of their stage shows are Satanic rituals live in front of 20,000 people without them realizing and recognizing.”

Shane, a Pentecostal born-again Christian, further suggested the hoods, masks, capes, and even the shape of the stage are part of the ritual. 

He added: “Even down to Taylor Swift – one of the biggest artists in the world – you watch one of her shows and she has two or three different demonic rituals to do with the pentagrams on the ground, to do with all sorts of stuff on her stage.

“But to a lot of people, it’s just art and that’s how people are seeing it, unfortunately.”

This isn’t the first time

Shockingly, Taylor has actually been accused of whipping out witchcraft before.

Earlier this month, some fans suggested the singer had made “the sign of the horns” while performing Cruel Summer. 

On TikTok, content creators further claimed Taylor was harnessing “dark energy” in her music to entrap the souls of her fans. The same accounts also speculate about the star being a clone, so I wouldn’t pledge your soul to Satan too soon, Swifties.

Celebrity Tidbit and GRV Media have approached Taylor Swift’s representatives for comment.
