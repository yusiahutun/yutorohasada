Jennifer Garner steals the show in tightfitting outfit at Daily Front Row Fashion Awards

 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  4 minutes  |  724 words  |  Martina Birk

    Jennifer Garner truly dazzled as she embraced full-on glam for the Daily Front Row's Eighth Annual Fashion Los Angeles Awards, held at the iconic Beverly Hills Hotel, and where Kris Jenner, Sarah Michelle Gellar and more were on hand to honor those behind the cameras.

    The alum, 52, looked every inch the star in a striking ensemble that was a departure from her usual casual wear, turning heads in a stunning baby-blue corseted bodice that elegantly bared her shoulders, perfectly paired with a maroon tea-length skirt. 

    The highlight of Jennifer's evening was presenting the award for Hairstylist of the Year to her go-to stylist since 2001, Adir Abergel. 

    Known for his exceptional talent and heartfelt approach to his craft, Adir wore a dramatic black pantsuit with massive shoulder pads and white external boning, complemented by sky-high platform booties. His unique style perfectly mirrored the innovative spirit of the night.

    Their close relationship was evident as they shared laughs and affectionate moments on the red carpet, where they affectionately referred to each other as 'Boogie,' and in her speech introducing Adir, she praised his work, quipping how he was able to work with an "all-American" actress like her as well as others who happily chose bolder looks.

    Sarah Michelle looked gorgeous in a sequin mini dress, taking to the stage to introduce Make Up Artist of the Year Rachel Goodwin, and joking that she had traveled to more countries with Rachel than with her own husband of over 20 years, Freddie Prinze Jr.

    Doja Cat, who HELLO! spotted sitting next to Jennifer and exclaiming that she would love to have her picture with the actress, was joined by her friend Brett Alan Nelson, who was honored with the Music Stylist of the Year award.

    The event featured a cocktail party hosted by Casamigos, followed by an award ceremony emceed by Celeste Barber, which saw Anastasia Soare honored with the Beauty Innovator award, presented by Kris Jenner, and Amelia Gray for Model of the Year award, given to her by mom Lisa Rinna.

    Bob Mackie was presented with the Life Time Achievement award from Law Roach.

    Jen's look was beautifully curated by stylist Jordan Johnson Chung, who completed the outfit with matching strappy stiletto heels, adding just the right touch of sophistication and charm. 

    Her accessories were equally glamorous, featuring a maroon Métier London clutch, sparkly earrings, and a bold statement bracelet that added a dash of sparkle to her evening attire.

    The mom of three opted for minimal makeup and styled her shoulder-length brunette locks in a chic wet look, showcasing her natural beauty and understated elegance.

    Away from the glitz of the awards night, Jennifer is busy raising children Violet, 18, Fin, 15, and Samuel, 12, and mourning the loss of her beloved father, Bill, who passed away on March 30 at age 85.  

    Professionally, she's thrilled to continue her role as executive producer and star in the second season of , which despite mixed reviews, emerged as Apple TV+'s most-watched limited series within its first 31 days, drawing an impressive 4.5 million viewers. Excited about the series' renewal, she recently expressed on Instagram, "I'm dying to know what happens to Hannah and Bailey. Thank you for ordering another season of #TheLastThingHeToldMe, @appletv."

    Adding to her packed schedule, Jennifer is set to reprise her role as the billionaire heiress-turned-mercenary Elektra Natchios in the upcoming MCU blockbuster D. 

    The highly anticipated film, hitting theaters on July 26, will see her star alongside the likes of Ryan Reynolds, Hugh Jackman and Emma Corrin. Jennifer's portrayal of the sai-swinging seductress in (2003) and its sequel (2005) not only marked a significant chapter in her career but also introduced her to her former husband and two-time Oscar-winning filmmaker, Ben Affleck.

    In addition to her exciting roles in high-profile films, Jennifer continues to expand her creative horizons. She is involved in producing and starring in the Netflix holiday comedy s, and will appear in Max Winkler's true-crime film alongside Paul Walter Hauser. 

    As a KitchenAid paid partner, she seamlessly integrates her passion for cooking with her professional projects, further highlighting her versatility and appeal.
