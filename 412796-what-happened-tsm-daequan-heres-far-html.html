Daequan was born on May 12th, 1994, in Florida, in the United States. He became a Team SoloMid (TSM) member after signing with them to be part of their competitive Fortnite squad. He rose to fame when he won the first-ever Fortnite Invitational back in 2017.

Who is TSM Daequan?

TSM Daequan's real name is Daequan Loco. He is known for establishing the double-pump meta and significantly using it when the community was still learning how to play the game. His skill eventually led to him becoming a founding member of TSM's competitive Fortnite team.
