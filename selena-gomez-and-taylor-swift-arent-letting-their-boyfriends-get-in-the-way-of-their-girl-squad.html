Selena Gomez and Taylor Swift aren't letting their boyfriends get in the way of the girl squad

 Posted on May 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  407 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Selena Gomez and Taylor Swift have once again been spotted on a night out together in New York City, after the Disney Channel alum confirmed her romance with producer Benny Blanco.

The music stars were joined alongside celebrity friends including Cara Delevingne, Anya Taylor-Joy, and Zoë Kravitz. Selena Gomez looked stylish and happy as she showed off her new blonde highlights. New man and new hair, Selena’s entering a new era.

Selena Gomez and Taylor Swift step out for ‘taken girls night out’

Taking to the cold streets of New York, the Single Soon singer joined Taylor Swift as they headed to a pizza joint.

The 31-year-old stunned in a snake-print mini skirt with a matching floor-length coat. Adding to the winter style, she paired the skirt with a pair of tights and stylish heeled knee-high black boots.

Her hair was placed into effortless waves as she showed off her new blonde highlights which she recently debuted.

Gomez’s outing comes after she confirmed she was dating Benny Blanco via comments on an Instagram post.

It seems that the singer and producer have been dating for six months, and have been keeping things on the down low.

The music star gushed about her new boyfriend in the ‘announcement’ via the comments, as she wrote: “He is my absolute everything in my heart.”

She even seemed to take a swipe at her exes as she said that she would never “be with a f***boy again.”

Fans loved seeing the celebrity besties together even though they both seemed loved up right now, as one exclaimed: “The girl squad lives on!”

Taylor has been dating NFL star Travis Kelce since July, before going public in September, when the Love Story singer attended a Kansas City Chiefs game.

The besties met through their exes

The celeb besties have been going strong since 2008, and they actually met through their exes.

Although it now feels like a lifetime ago, around that time Taylor was dating Joe Jonas. Selena was dating his younger brother Nick.

Reflecting on the relationships with Kiss FM in 2017, the Wizards of Waverley Place alum called the time “hysterical”.

“It was amazing, because [Taylor] was the girl with the big curly hair and all the bracelets and the cowboy boots. And I was definitely up-and-coming, and we just clicked,” she said.

“It was the best thing we got out of those relationships,” she joked.
