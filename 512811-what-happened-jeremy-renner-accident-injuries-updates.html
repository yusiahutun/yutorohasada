Who is Jeremy Renner?

Jeremy is one of the best American actors. He was born on 7th January 1971 in Modesto, California, USA. How old is Jeremy Renner? He is 52 years old as of 2023.

He is the son of Lee Renner and Valerie Cearley. His parents married as teenagers but divorced when he was only ten. He is the firstborn of seven siblings.

After primary school, he graduated from Fred C Beyer High School in Modesto in 1989. Later, he joined Modesto Junior College to study computer science and criminology. He took drama class as an elective in college but pursued acting.

What happened to Jeremy Renner?

What actually happened to Jeremy Renner? The actor was involved in a tragic snowplough accident on New Year’s Day. It all began when he towed his nephew’s truck off the driveway after being stuck in the snow. His snowplough started rolling down the hill after successfully towing the truck into the street.
