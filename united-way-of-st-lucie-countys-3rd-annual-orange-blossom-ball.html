United Way of St. Lucie Countys 3rd Annual Orange Blossom Ball

 Posted on June 13, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  57 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Veronica Kolibab, Monte Kosoff and Grace Vergis

Harbour Ridge Yacht & Country Club was rich with Parisian fashion and décor for the elegant “Evening in Paris” themed event. Guests were greeted at the door with a signature cocktail, Le Kir Royale L’Orange. Guests enjoyed French cuisine, a silent auction, live music and dancing throughout the evening.
