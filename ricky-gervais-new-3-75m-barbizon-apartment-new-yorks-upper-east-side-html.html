Luxury: Ricky Gervais and partner Jane Fallon paid £2.5million for their second apartment in New York

Grace Kelly apparently danced down the halls in just her underwear, and Liza Minnelli and Joan Crawford also called it home.

But the residential hotel which was built for women in the 1920s is slowly being taken over by someone a little less glamorous - Ricky Gervais.

The comedian has bought his second apartment in The Barbizon, an exclusive building on New York's Upper East Side.

He and and his producer partner Jane Fallon have splashed out £2.5million on their luxury home on the 12th floor.

The 1,794-square-foot, newly renovated corner apartment boasts two bedrooms and two-and-a-half bathrooms.

The real estate listing says it features 'stunning open city views throughout'.

It has two balconies, a dining room, laundry room and kitchen.

And there will be no excuse for Ricky not to keep up his impressive fitness regime.

The home has a private entrance to the Equinox gym which has a pool and spa.

The apartment plays it safe with a beige decor but no doubt Ricky and Jane will add their own touch.

Moving up in the world: Inside Ricky's apartment on the twelfth floor, three floors above his $1.66million (£1.1million) unit her bought in 2008

Stunning: The comedian's new place boasts wooden floors and has been newly renovated, according to its real estate listing

The vendors are Patrick and Ita Chesser who listed County Limerick, Ireland as their address when they bought the apartment in 2007.
