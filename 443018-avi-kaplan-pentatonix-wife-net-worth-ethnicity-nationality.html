Early life

Avi Benjamin Kaplan was born on 17 April 1989 (age 32) in Visalia, California, United States. He is the son of Michael Kaplan (father) and Shelly Kaplan (mother) and has two siblings, Esther Kaplan Koop and Joshua Kaplan.

Avriel Kaplan grew up in Visalia, where he attended school. He went to school at Divisadero Middle School before joining Mt. Whitney High School. After graduating from high school, Avi went to study at Mt. San Antonio College.

While growing up, Avi Kaplan loved listening to folk music. As a result, he frequented Sequoia National Park near his home in the southern Sierra Nevada east of Visalia, California. The park became his inspiration, and he knew he wanted to be a musician in future. It is also where he found escape and discovered his taste for a cappella.
