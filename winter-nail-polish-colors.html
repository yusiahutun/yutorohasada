The 10 Best Nail Polish Colors for Your Winter Manicures

 Posted on September 29, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  608 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Having to go out when it's so cold that I need a few layers under my parka is not my idea of a good time.

Instead, I prefer to spend winter at home, wrapped in a blanket and on my couch like a warm, toasty burrito. And my cold weather staying-in routine involves trashy reality shows, Seamless orders, and a ton of DIY manicures.

It might be cozy in my apartment, but throughout winter my nail polish choices are like the weather: chill. Those neons and pastels I wore during summer and into fall suddenly don't feel right anymore, so I rotate between a handful of deep jewel tones and rich seasonal shades. Sometimes, I throw in a little bit of glitter in for good measure.

From rusty red-orange to chrome, and every dark shade in-between, keep reading for the 10 best nail polish colors to wear for winter.

Sally Hansen Color Foil Nail Polish in Steel a Kiss

Get the look and feel of a trendy chrome manicure without the mess (or a trip to the nail salon). This Sally Hansen polish offers a high shine look in just two coats.

Essie Nail Polish in Carols & Caviar

With the cooler temperatures and shorter days, it's inevitable that you'll look to the winter weather for inspiration for your manicure. That's where a rich, creamy navy blue nail polish comes in, which channels the midnight sky during a night of winter revelry.

OPI Infinite Shine Long-Wear Lacquer in Rhinestone Red-y

It's impossible not to feel festive when you have a red shimmer polish on your nails. For all of your holiday functions, we present you with this sparkly ruby OPI gel-finish formula.

JINsoon Nail Polish in Fire Clay

In lieu of a classic bold red or subtle beige, opt for an earthy in-between shade like this rusty brown Earth tone from JINsoon. The pop of color will compliment all of the deep neutrals in your winter wardrobe.

Chanel Le Vernis in Émotion

Level up a neutral manicure with a creamy pink polish. This muted rose Chanel shade has a long-wear formula that's infused with bioceramics and ceramides to nourish and strengthen the nails.

Morgan Taylor I Wanna Dance With Somebody Collection in Record Breaker

Yep, black nail polish can be festive when infused with tiny flecks of glitter. Another reason to celebrate: Morgan Taylor's polishes stay fresh for up to 10 days post-DIY manicure and come with a cuticle-friendly brush that makes even application a breeze.

Sundays Studio in No.21

This cool-toned grape polish is a seasonal way to wear purple, a color that dominated both makeup and manicure trends this year.

Nails.Inc Plant Power Vegan Nail Polish in Wipe The Slate Green

Green isn't a nail polish color exclusive to St. Patrick's Day, and the proof is in this wintery forest green polish from Nails.Inc. The formula is also unique: It's completely vegan, made with 73% plant-based ingredients, and 21-free.

Cirque Nail Polish in Moonbeam

The glazed donut manicure trend isn't going anywhere, and winter is arguably the best time to embrace the look. The sheer, iridescent glitter mimics the frost and ice shimmering in the sunlight. Nail the look at home with a white holographic glitter nail polish like this one from Cirque Colors.

Miniluxe Nail Polish in Champagne

The party doesn't have to stop on January 1st – at least as far as your manicures goes. Inject a little sparkle into your mid-winter looks with a shimmery champagne manicure. This Miniluxe shade with flecks of silver glitter will do just the trick.
