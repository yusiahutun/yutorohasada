Eliza Dushku welcomes second son Gregory Bodan 'Bodie' with husband Peter Palandjian

 Posted on June 27, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  521 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Buffy the Vampire Slayer alum Eliza Dushku announced Monday that she welcomed her second child - son Gregory Bodan 'Bodie' - with husband, Intercontinental Real Estate Corporation CEO Peter Palandjian.

'Welcoming our beautiful son "Bodan" this week has been a journey in [and] of itself,' the half-Albanian, half-Danish 40-year-old - who boasts 3.5M social media followers - wrote.

'Gratitude [and] joy! These boys give life new horizons. Bourne & Bodie: #Boston brothers. Thanks to all out there who support [and] love us. Ah, this is where we're supposed to be.'

Eliza and the 57-year-old real estate executive were already parents to two-year-old son Philip 'Bourne,' who's excited to be a big brother.
