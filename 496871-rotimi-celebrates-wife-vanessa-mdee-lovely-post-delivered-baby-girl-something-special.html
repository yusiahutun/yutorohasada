Rotimi Vanessa Mdee's 2nd child

Rotimi says women are stronger

On Friday, March 3, TUKO.co.ke reported that Vanessa and Rotimi were blessed with another child, and he couldn't stop gushing over her birthing experience.

Rotimi took to Instagram to compliment his lover and women in general for their strength in bringing life to the world.

"I admire you all. This is just incredible just to watch how strong you are and how you handling this. Contractions. Man, as men we can't handle that. We can barely handle certain massages. This is crazy. To all the mothers out there, wow (while saluting), wow. You guys need all the praise," he said.
