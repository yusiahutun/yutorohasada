Love is in the air in Coronation Street, as Fiz Stape and Phill Whittaker prepare to be married.

Their road to romance has had its ups and downs, but will Fiz finally find her forever after? Viewers will have to wait and see.

Actress Jennie McAlpine looked radiant as she was snapped filming her Coronation Street wedding scenes last month.

It is one of a number of glamorous looks that Jennie has flaunted in recent years - both on screen and off.

As her onscreen counterpart prepares to marry, Daily Star takes a look at Jennie McAlpine's most glamorous looks.

Corrie corset

Jennie McAlpine turned up the heat on the cobbles, when she rocked a sexy skintight corset in Coronation Street.

The sultry number featured red lace and satin detailing on the torso, with the busty number curving over Jennie's hips for an extra sexy twist.

Showing off Jennie's killer curves, the actress looked incredible in one of Fiz's hottest outfits during her time on the soap.

I'm A Celeb stint

Many soap stars have entered the jungle for I'm A Celebrity...Get Me Out Of Here! in recent years, including Jennie's Corrie co-star Simon Gregson.

Looking ready for action, Jennie looked incredible as she donned a wide brimmed hat, t-shirt, shirt tied in a sexy knot at the front and tiny red shorts.

The star prepared to take on the jungle in the 2017 series of the ITV competition, with her sailing through the challenges to come fourth in the series.

Red carpet glamour

Jennie McAlpine has flaunted a number of sensational looks at red carpet events in recent years, including her eye-popping blue jumpsuit at the British Soap Awards 2017.

The jumpsuit featured a lace bodice that was cinched at the waist to show off Jennie's gorgeous figure, while the satin trousers flowed off her killer curves.

Paired with matching midnight blue heels, this is one of our favourite red carpet looks to date from the star.

Pretty in pink

Jennie McAlpine looked radiant as she posed for photographs at the National Television Awards 2018.

The star wore a hot pink bardot dress, with the sleeveless look showing off her toned arms and torso, paired with grey heels for a quirky addition to the outfit.

With her red locks flowing past her shoulders, Jennie was the very essence of glam in the eye-popping look.

Golden icon

Jennie brought Hollywood glamour to the red carpet in 2013, as the soap icon became a fashion icon in a dazzling golden outfit at the British Soap Awards.

Dripped head to toe in a gold sleeveless dress, the outfit's stunning details including a ruffle on one shoulder and an asymmetric neckline flattered Jennie's beautiful figure.

Teamed with silver eyeshadow that framed her gorgeous eyes, Jennie looked every inch the acting icon that she is.

Photoshoot ready

Away from the red carpet and the cobbles, Jennie McApline has showcased a number of stunning looks in photoshoots too.

Posing for OK! Magazine, Jennie looked beautiful in a white crochet midi dress, with the star adding a pop of colour with red high heels for a chic finish.

Looking relaxed and refined, the actress looked simply sensational in the look - and gave us all outfit envy in the process.
