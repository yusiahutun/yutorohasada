Yellowstone stars Hassie Harrison and Ryan Bingham confirm relationship

 Posted on July 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  368 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Yellowstone actors Hassie Harrison and Ryan Bingham have officially confirmed their romantic relationship, posting an endearing yet fiery photo on Instagram on Wednesday. 

The 42-year-old actor shared a picture of the couple sharing a passionate kiss while standing in front of a blazing fire.

In the captivating photo, both Hassie, 33, and Ryan donned matching camouflage jackets, jeans, and dark boots, with caps tilted upward as they leaned in for the kiss. 

Ryan made a reference to the blazing inferno behind them in his caption, writing: "More than a spark," and tagging Hassie. She responded in the comments, saying: "I love you, cowboy," accompanied by a heart-eyed emoji.

Both actors are relatively new additions to the Yellowstone cast. Hassie joined the series in its third season in 2020, playing Laramie, a ranch hand and horse racer working for John Dutton III, portrayed by Kevin Costner. Ryan, who began his career as a singer-songwriter, has been part of the show since its inception. 

He plays Walker, another ranch hand who is also a musician and former convict. Initially a recurring character, Walker became a series regular in season four.

Interestingly, Hassie and Ryan's off-screen romance mirror their characters' relationship in Yellowstone.

Hassie was previously involved with actor Austin Nichols, while Ryan shares three children with ex-wife Anna Axster, to whom he was married from 2009 to 2021.

The actors' romance announcement comes amid uncertainty surrounding the future of the popular series. 

Kevin is reportedly seeking to reduce his shooting commitments for the show's upcoming fifth season to focus on directing and starring in his multi-part western epic, Horizon. 

This development has fueled speculation that series creator and showrunner Taylor Sheridan might write off Kevin's character. 

Meanwhile, The Hollywood Reporter has confirmed that Matthew McConaughey will star in his own Yellowstone spinoff series.
