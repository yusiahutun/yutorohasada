Most daring eating challenges in UK - including baby-sized burrito and 96oz steak

 Posted on August 23, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  655 words  |  Martina Birk

People often brag amongst mates that they could win any eating challenge there is. Now they have the opportunity to put their money where their mouth is

2Chill has created a list of the biggest and boldest eating challenges you can try out, all of them in Bristol.

This list includes some heavy-hitting meals, like a burrito the same weight as a baby and a 96oz steak.

El Criminal, Mission Burrito

62 Park St, Bristol, BS1 5JN

This Mexican eating challenge is enough to make you say “¡Hasta luego!” to burritos for life!

Equal to the weight of a newborn baby, Mission Burrito’s 5lb colossal burrito is loaded with three meats, rice, beans, pico de gallo, lettuce and cheese, and “smothered like only a criminal would with sour cream and salsas”.

Because nobody ever says no to nachos, the hot ‘n’ spicy burrito is served on a foot-long bed of nacho chips. If you fail to finish every last one, you will have to pay £25.

Complete it, and you’ll be rewarded with a T-shirt and a claim to fame on the Heroes Roll of Honour. So far, only one person has finished El Criminal in Bristol. Could you be the second?

Go face to face (or mouth to tortilla) with the biggest and hottest burrito in Bristol at Mission Burrito.

96oz Steak Challenge, The Ashville Steakhouse

15 Leigh St, Southville, Bristol, BS3 1SN

Talk about meat sweats!

This challenge is one you cannot underestimate. It is so mighty and meaty that two of the world’s strongest men have even failed.

To beat their attempt, you will need to stomach a 96oz steak, with five sides of onion rings, triple cooked chips, garlic bread, vegetables and salad, topped with peppercorn and Béarnaise sauce.

The 96oz Steak Challenge costs £110 to take on, or it’s free for those that can wash it down with a pint in under an hour!

So many people have tried to defeat it, but only one person has succeeded - a professional eater from the Czech Republic.

If you think you’ve got what it takes to beat the world’s strongest men, visit The Ashville Steakhouse .

BBQ Challenge, BARE Grills

41 Triangle W, Clifton, Bristol, BS8 1ER

Only one person in the world has completed this Bristol challenge - and it wasn’t easy for professional eater Beard Meets Food.

You have one hour to finish: a BARE Grill burger, full rack of ribs, smoked brisket, pulled pork, chicken wings, smoked sausage, dirty bacon fries, onion rings, dirty burnt end fries, two bread rolls and two corn on the cobs. Can you do it?

If you polish off everything on the plate (excluding bones) within the 60-minute time frame, you get your meal for free. If you fail, it’s £45!

And don’t try to bring an eating buddy - this one’s all on your own.

No cheating, no substitutions and no swaps. The decision of the Pitmaster is final!

To take on the challenge, all you need to do is let them know in advance and book yourself a table at the BARE Grills Bristol restaurant.

St Philip’s Causeway, Bristol, BS4 3BD

One person, five sauces, 25 wings. Do you dare to take on the challenge?

The new Flaming Grill Who Dares Wings challenge has 25 wings tossed in five flavours - Texan Style BBQ, Piri Piri, Sweet Chilli, Spicy Dry Rub and TABASCO Scorpion Sauce.

It’s sure to tingle your taste buds!

The wings must be eaten by one individual in one sitting. Wing-ers will receive a certificate or be featured on the Wall of Fame.

If you’ve got leftovers, don’t try to argue it - manager’s decision is final!
