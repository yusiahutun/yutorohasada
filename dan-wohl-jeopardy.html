Early Life

A resident of Forest Hills, Queens, Dan was born in 1987 and he is a native of California. He has the origin of his last name in the German language but belongs to American descent. As per his Facebook profile, Danielle Wohl is his mother while Gary Wohl is his father and Andrew Wohl is his younger brother. From his LinkedIn profile, he completed his bachelor’s from the University of Wisconsin-Madison, majoring in Political Science from 2004 to 2008. Later, he went to Stanford University and received a degree in M.A. (Secondary Education and Teaching).

Dan Wohl Career

He has experienced almost every private practice including radio, media, marketing and communications, editorial, producing, writing, marketing manager, and finally as a teacher. He began his professional life while working in the Office of Governor Jim Doyle as a “Constituent Services Intern”. For another four months, he was interned as an “Opinion Intern” at Wisconsin State Journal.

For the next three years, he joined The Daily Cardinal and attained the position of “Arts Editor/Film and Music Critic”. He has been working professionally since 2007, even when he did not complete his graduating degree in B.A. From September 2007 to May 2008, he interned at the Office of Senator Herb Kohl, based in Medison, Wisconsin Area. In total, he gained 17 experiences from different companies having different positions, including-
