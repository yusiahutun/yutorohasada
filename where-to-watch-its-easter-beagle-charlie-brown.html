Spring is here! And that means Easter celebrations have also arrived. Whether or not you have plans this weekend to observe the holiday, don’t forget to stream yet another Charlie Brown special: It’s the Easter Beagle Charlie Brown. The Charlie Brown Easter episode has officially landed on Apple TV for all to watch at home.

It’s the Easter Beagle Charlie Brown is the 12th prime-time animated TV special based on the comic strip Peanuts by Charles M. Schulz. It received an Emmy nomination for Outstanding Children’s Special in 1975, along with Be My Valentine, Charlie Brown that same year.

The Easter special mainly centers around the “Easter Beagle” who will take care of all festivities, according to Linus. Watch as Peppermint Patty and Marcie attempt to color Easter eggs, Snoopy helps Woodstock buy a birdhouse, and see who receives an Easter egg from the elusive “Easter Beagle.” 

The holiday special is one of the many Charlie Brown TV editions. Other TV specials featuring Peanuts characters include A Charlie Brown Thanksgiving, It’s the Great Pumpkin, Snoopy, Come Home, You’re Not Elected, Charlie Brown, and There’s No Time for Love, Charlie Brown, among others.

If you’re in the mood for an Easter classic, you’re likely trying to figure out how you can watch It’s the Easter Beagle Charlie Brown with your family or solo at home. Keep scrolling to discover where to watch It’s the Easter Beagle Charlie Brown for free to see Charlie and his crew discover the true meaning of Easter.

Where to watch It’s the Easter Beagle Charlie Brown for free

It’s the Easter Beagle Charlie Brown is available to stream on Apple TV Plus, which offers a seven-day free trial and costs $9.99 per month after the trial ends. Along with It’s the Easter Beagle Charlie Brown, Apple TV Plus also includes dozens of Apple Originals with new content add each month. Apple Originals include award-winning comedy and drama series such as Ted Lasso, Severance, The Morning Show and Bad Sisters, as well as original movies, documentaries, and children’s content. The service also offers other Peanuts specials such as A Charlie Brown Thanksgiving, It’s the Great Pumpkin, Charlie Brown, and Be My Valentine, Charlie Brown. Apple TV Plus additionally allows users to stream on up to five devices and download content to watch offline.

How to watch It’s the Easter Beagle Charlie Brown for free

Read on for step-by-step instructions on how to watch It’s the Easter Beagle Charlie Brown on Apple TV Plus.

  • Visit TV.Apple.com
  • Click “Try It Free“
  • Create an account with your email or Apple ID
  • Enter your information and payment method
