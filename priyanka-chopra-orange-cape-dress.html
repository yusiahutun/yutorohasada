Priyanka Chopra makes a case for the cape dress

 Posted on February 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  232 words  |  Aldo Pusey

You can always rely on Priyanka Chopra to create a little drama on the red carpet and last night was no different; the actress attended a Bumble event in Mumbai wearing a bright orange caped dress with matching tangerine shoes.

The fitted dress featured shoulder pads and exaggerated, long, pleated sleeves which resembled a cape. Designed by Australian label Alex Perry, the dress has actually already been seen on more than a few A-listers.

SUJIT JAISWAL//Getty Images

Lupita Nyong'o wore a one-shouldered version of the design in a pale-pink hue for a 'Women in Hollywood' event a few months ago. Meanwhile, Ivanka Trump opted for the same style as Chopra in pale pink for an engagement recently and Chanel Iman wore it in a neon yellow in January.
