Sarah Michelle Gellar looks stunning in sultry shoot for the cover of NewBeauty magazine

 Posted on June 10, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  776 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Sarah Michelle Gellar looked stunning on the latest cover of NewBeauty magazine.

For one look, the 45-year-old Buffy the Vampire Slayer actress showed off her curves in a plunging, tight-fitting Versace dress as she posed sultrily in the frame of a floor-to-ceiling window.

In another, the Cruel Intentions star sported a black mesh bra underneath an unbuttoned beige blouse paired with off-white silk pants as she smiled brightly for the camera.

The blonde beauty — who modeled a sheer, lace minidress while attending the screening for Wolf Pack — modeled various other classic looks remixed with edgy details and rocked a bold, coral lip.

In the interview, the Scooby-Doo alum talked about ageing.

'It can be hard when you've been in the public eye for a long time, but I think that I'm the worst at comparing myself to what I used to look like,' Gellar said.

'I'm harder on myself than the public is,' she continued.

'I can't expect to look like I did when I was 20, because that's just ridiculous and unrealistic.' 

In the interview with the magazine, the Scream vet — who has kids Charlotte, 13, and Rocky, 10 with husband Freddie Prinze Jr — said she likes to care of her mental health.

'I sort of realized during the pandemic that maybe I wasn't taking care of myself as well as I could,' she said.

'I keep telling my husband that I feel like I've aged so much in those three years of having the kids home and homeschooling and all that!' she noted.

On her husband's response, she said: 'He keeps saying, "You didn't age anymore in those three years than any other three years."

'But it sure feels like I did!

For the stunning shoot, Gellar styled her shoulder-length, blonde hair in casual beach waves and let her outfits hold the center of attention.

On the cover of the magazine, she donned an oversized white peplum top paired with the matching miniskirt and platform heels while looking out the window, deep in thought.

One of her most striking looks, however, was a black, long-sleeved top remixed with a one-shoulder cutout and a pair of simple black slacks.

The I Know What You Did Last Summer actress opted for minimal jewelry minus zigzag earrings and a statement ring with a white organically-shaped circle.

She looked radiant in the portraits in a classic makeup look that highlighted her natural and youthful beauty.
