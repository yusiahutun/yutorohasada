Selena Gomez Is a Boss in This Floor-Sweeping Faux-Leather Trench Coat

 Posted on September 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  331 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Selena Gomez exuded boss energy while out in New York City this weekend amid the cloudy early-spring weather.

The actor and singer went out for a late dinner at celebrity-loved hot spot Carbone on Sunday, and for the occasion, wrapped herself up in the coolest floor-length trench. The black faux-leather coat has a sharp collar and black buttons, and it hails from model Elsa Hosk’s fashion label, Helsa. Since Hosk launched it in late 2022, the brand has released various It girl pieces that have instantly gone viral—like the romantic sheer white dress once worn by Kendall Jenner, or the chic black slipdress with a white rosette that Elle Fanning stepped out in on a date night.

BACKGRID

Gomez layered her bold outerwear (which retails for $598) over a black top and breezy black trousers, and finished her look with pointy black leather boots. As for accessories, the “Lose You to Love Me” singer carried a glossy black square-shaped bag and added some round silver shades with red ombré lenses, plus dainty gold jewelry. She had her brown hair slicked back into a side-parted bun, with sideswept bangs swishing over her face.

Gomez’s all-black outfit offered a sharp contrast to her look on Saturday, which was monochrome pink. She wore the ensemble—a flirty button-up dress with a matching wool coat and pointy heels—to an event celebrating her cosmetic brand Rare Beauty’s new blush collection, held at Manhattan’s Studio 525.
