Taylor Swifts Casual Summer Look Includes Maxi Dress, Chunky Sandals

 Posted on August 31, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  342 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Taylor Swift’s latest look is a lesson in casual, chic summer dressing.

The singer was spotted out in New York City yesterday wearing a breezy black maxi dress, which she cinched at the waist with a matching leather belt with a golden buckle. She paired the piece with chunky black leather sandals with golden buckles by Anine Bing, a sand-colored leather shoulder bag by Mansur Gavriel, a Cartier silver-and-gold chain necklace, small gold hoop earrings, and black tortoiseshell sunglasses (also by Anine Bing).

She added a pop of color to the monochrome look with her signature bright red lip and had her hair up in a messy bun.

Gotham//Getty Images

The “Karma” singer has been spending plenty of time in New York between Eras Tour shows. In April, she was pictured leaving hotspot Via Carota with friends in a black off-the-shoulder top and high-waisted cutoff blue jeans by Area, featuring a butterfly-shaped cutout embellished with clear crystals. She finished the look with glossy black heeled Mary Janes by Sam Edelman, a shimmering silver Paco Rabanne mini bag, and a silver necklace.

Late last month, she stepped out in Manhattan in an airy all-white spring look and her iconic red lip. The outfit was composed of a white eyelet Ralph Lauren bustier top and a matching midi skirt. She finished the laid-back look with strappy caramel-colored sandals and a Mansur Gavriel shoulder bag in the shade “desert.”

And days prior, Swift was seen out in a classic white button-down shirt, ripped denim jeans, and the same strappy sandals—a look fans quickly suggested contained a big message about the trending topic “Leave her alone” on Swiftie Twitter.
