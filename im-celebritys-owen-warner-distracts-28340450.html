I'm A Celebrity star Owen Warner left fans in a hot sweat with his recent Instagram snap.

The 23-year-old Hollyoaks hunk has recently landed in Australia as he prepares to join the cast of the gruelling ITV reality show.

Before heading Down Under, he posed up a storm for the camera to show off his ripped abs.

Unsurprisingly, his social media update caught the attention of his 255,000 followers, with some rushing to leave a flirty message for the hunk in the comments sections.

One fan penned: "Totally not zooming in."

Another added: "F'king hell" alongside the aubergine emoji.

A third person said: "Can almost see your religion."

While a fourth gushed: "I was definitely looking at your toes too."

Owen is best known for portraying the role of Romeo Nightingale in Hollyoaks, and at 23 is the youngest contestant to be announced for I'm A Celeb this year so far.

Asked if he was leaving any loved ones back home he said he would miss his family and friends, but was single at the moment.

He said: "My mum is the one who is going to be the one missing me. I am single and ready for the jungle. I always looking for romance but I am not going in especially for that.

"I will miss football and family most."

Owen said some of the cast of Hollyoaks knew he was going and had told him to "enjoy it".

Love Island beauty Olivia Attwood, radio presenter Chris Moyles and Coronation Street star Sue Cleaver have also touched down in Oz ready to take on the Bushtucker Trials.

The series will kick off on Sunday, November 6 with ITV bosses bringing the start date forward in order to make way for the World Cup later on in the month.
