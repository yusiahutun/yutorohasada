Bella Hadid looks cool in 3,030 studded belt from her Chrome Hearts x Bella collection

 Posted on March 5, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  443 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The 20-year-old model took to Instagram to showcase the design

    Back in February, Bella Hadid’s much-anticipated collaboration with LA label Chrome Hearts was debuted at Paris Fashion Week, and the fashion-forward model is quite rightly a fan of her ultra-cool range. Most recently, the 20-year-old was spotted wearing a patent black studded belt from the 40-piece collection in a photo on her Instagram account.

    Captioning the shot, in which she is seen wearing a form-fitting white off-the-shoulder bodysuit tucked into an oversized pair of camouflage trousers and of course, the belt, she wrote: "My apartment is starting to overflow (in my #chromeheartsxbella studded belt)."

    STORY: Bella Hadid steps out in Paris in cool £65 Nike trainers 

    Bella Hadid modelled a studded belt from her Chrome Hearts x Bella collection

    Bella looked fabulous as she posed in her walk-in-wardrobe, which indeed was overflowing, but neatly so, with a row of colour-coordinated coats in black and orange hues to her left, while a row of crisp white trainers and another rail of clothes are seen to her right.

    Looking casual cool in the photo, Bella, wore her glossy brown hair in a swept back in a sleek ‘do, The off-duty model looked pretty with minimal make-up. A sweep of bronzer highlighted her enviable cheekbones, while a strong brow, layers of black mascara and nude lipstick completed her look.

    View post on Instagram 

    Bella announced her collaboration with Chrome Hearts on Instagram back in Decemeber 

    Bella teased fans with the collaboration back in December last year, after taking to Instagram writing: "You have no idea what’s coming!" The 90s-inspired collection consists of cool leather mini skirts, jackets trimmed in pink faux fur, oversized parka jackets and cool cropped tees, and made its UK debut in May at London department store Selfridges.

    Bella accessorised her look with a white hat from her collection while out in NYC 

    Bella, who models for the collection, is often spotted wearing pieces from the range. Back in March, she strolled around New York in a white hat from her collaboration, which worked as the perfect accessory to her effortlessly chic outfit.

    Known for her edgy style, Bella opted for a simple white vest; teamed with an oversized knit, flared jeans and grey leg-lengthening boots. A pair of tinted aviator sunglasses and a Givenchy Lucrezia duffle bag were the perfect finishing touches to her ensemble.
