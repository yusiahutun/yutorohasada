It is not easy maintaining the sort of bikini figure that wins you a place on the cast of Baywatch.

But 33-year-old model Brooke Burns was looking as good as ever as she larked around with her film director husband in Hawaii today

The actress, who played Jessie Owens in the hit lifeguard show, looked fantastic as she dared to bare in a multicoloured two-piece.

She's still ready: Brooke Burns looks good enough to walk straight onto the set of Baywatch as she enjoyed the tropical sunshine in Hawaii today

The actress has struggled to land such a high profile acting spot the program finished in 2001, but she has certainly not lost any of her original charms.

She last appeared in the direct-to-DVD flick Titanic II, which traced the disastrous journey of a boat named after the tragic cruise ship in 2012.

Most recently she appeared as a presenter on ABC game show You Deserve It, in which contestents can win up to $250,000.

However she has had far more luck in attracting high-profile boyfriends, as after divorcing Fantastic Four star Julian McMahon in 2001, she ended up dating action hero Bruce Willis for 11 months in 2004.

And her latest beau is multi-talented Warrior director Gavin O'Conner, who also works as a screenwriter and producer.

The pair shared a chuckle as they enjoyed the sun in the tropical paradise, with the mother-of-one obviously enjoying some holiday time alone with her fiance.

Maybe Gavin could give Brooke a leg up by offering her a role in his next project, which he has yet to be announce.
