Beyonc Looked Incredible in a Short Mini Dress &amp; Strappy Sandals

 Posted on July 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  309 words  |  Larita Shotwell
  • Beyoncé stepped out on Saturday for Roc Nation's The Brunch, ahead of the Grammys.
  • The singer wore a chic minidress by Francesco Murano, with a pair of strappy sandals.
  • The star-studded event attracted a plethora of celebs including Rihanna, Kelly Rowland, and Sean "Diddy" Combs.

In a completely unsurprising turn of events, Beyoncé looked absolutely stunning at Roc Nation's annual pre-Grammys brunch.

Photographed at The Brunch alongside husband Jay-Z, Beyoncé was seriously chic in a gray minidress by Italian designer Francesco Murano. The dress was a perfect fit, and featured a revealing neckline and a short hem. She accessorized the sleek outfit with earrings by Lorraine Schwartz and strappy metallic sandals.

The superstar even packed on the PDA with her husband, and the pair coupled up for some adorable photos.

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

The "Halo" singer recently relaunched her athleisure line, Ivy Park, and was excited to share the new designs with her fans. Beyoncé wrote on Instagram, "I want to say a huge thank you to all of the incredible human beings who stood in those long lines in the snow and rain. All the beautiful people who waited in the waiting room online. All of the friends and family who took the time to film videos and dress up in the unboxing. I am humbled, grateful and proud. Y’all look so good in your IVY PARK. I love you deep. B."

A whole host of celebs rocked the new Ivy Park looks, with Reese Witherspoon showcasing pretty much the entire collection on Instagram.
